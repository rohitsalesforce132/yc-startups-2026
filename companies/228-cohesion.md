# Cohesion

**One-Liner:** Public equities agentic teammate

**Description:** Cohesion builds an AI agentic teammate for public equities investors that automates the tedious analytical work of investment research. Their platform deploys specialized agents that continuously monitor SEC filings, earnings calls, industry reports, and alternative data sources for specific stocks and sectors. When new information drops, Cohesion's agents automatically analyze the impact, update financial models, flag relevant precedents, and surface actionable insights in natural language. Portfolio managers and analysts interact with Cohesion as a tireless research partner that knows their portfolio, thesis, and risk parameters, enabling them to cover three to five times more names without adding headcount.
