# Framewise Health

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Healthcare
**Tags:** healthcare, patient-engagement, ai
**One-liner:** AI-native patient engagement

## What They Do

Framewise Health rebuilds patient communication from the ground up using AI. Current patient engagement tools send generic SMS reminders and blast emails. Framewise uses AI to personalize every interaction — understanding patient history, preferences, and behavior to send the right message at the right time through the right channel. It handles appointment reminders, follow-up care instructions, medication adherence, and preventive care nudges. For healthcare providers, this means better outcomes and fewer no-shows without hiring more staff.

Very high potential for India. Indian hospitals (Apollo, Fortis) and clinics struggle with patient follow-up. Build WhatsApp-native patient engagement for Indian healthcare. Revenue: ₹5K/month per hospital, 200 hospitals = ₹10L/month.
