# GovGuard

**One-Liner:** AI for government agencies

**Description:** GovGuard provides an AI platform that helps government agencies automate bureaucratic processes, manage constituent services, and streamline inter-departmental workflows. Their system handles incoming citizen requests through AI-powered intake, automatically routes them to the appropriate department, generates responses for common inquiries, and tracks resolution status. GovGuard also monitors regulatory compliance across agency operations, flagging potential issues before they become audit findings. The platform integrates with legacy government IT systems through secure APIs, meeting the stringent security and compliance requirements of federal, state, and local government agencies while modernizing their citizen-facing services.
