# Andco

**One-Liner:** End-to-end case workups for personal injury

**Description:** Andco automates the entire case workup process for personal injury law firms, from initial client intake through demand package generation. Their AI platform ingests police reports, medical records, billing statements, and insurance correspondence, then automatically builds comprehensive case timelines, calculates damages, identifies liable parties, and generates demand letters tailored to each insurance carrier's known negotiation patterns. Andco's models are trained on millions of settled PI cases, enabling accurate valuation and strategy recommendations. The platform transforms what takes paralegals weeks of document review into a same-day workflow, letting PI firms handle dramatically higher caseloads while improving settlement outcomes.
