# Orbit

**One-Liner:** AI community management platform

**Description:** Orbit provides an AI-powered community management platform that helps companies build, nurture, and scale their developer and user communities. Their AI automatically tracks member activity across GitHub, Discord, Slack, forums, and social media to measure engagement, identify emerging leaders, and surface at-risk members before they churn. Orbit's platform generates personalized outreach recommendations, automates welcome sequences for new members, and provides analytics dashboards that quantify community health and ROI. The AI identifies content gaps, suggests discussion topics that drive engagement, and matches community members for mentorship based on skill profiles. Community teams use Orbit to manage growing communities without proportionally growing their team size.
