# Monarch AI

**One-Liner:** AI financial planning and analysis

**Description:** Monarch AI provides an AI-powered FP&A platform that automates budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis for growing companies. Their platform connects to ERP, accounting, CRM, and HR systems to create real-time financial models that update automatically as underlying data changes. Monarch's AI generates multi-scenario forecasts, identifies variance drivers, and produces board-ready financial reports with narrative explanations of key trends. The platform replaces the Excel-heavy FP&A process that consumes finance teams' month-ends, providing always-current financial intelligence instead of stale spreadsheet snapshots. CFOs use Monarch to answer "what-if" questions in seconds, from headcount planning scenarios to revenue projection adjustments based on pipeline changes.
