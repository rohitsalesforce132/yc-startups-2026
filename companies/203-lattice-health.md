# Lattice Health

**One-Liner:** OS for clinical AI in imaging

**Description:** Lattice Health is building the operating system for deploying, managing, and monitoring AI models in clinical radiology settings. Their platform provides healthcare systems with a unified layer to run multiple FDA-cleared imaging AI tools across their radiology workflow — from chest X-rays to mammography to CT analysis. Lattice handles model versioning, performance monitoring against real-world data, integration with PACS systems, and audit trails for regulatory compliance. As hospitals struggle to manage an exploding ecosystem of point AI solutions, Lattice provides the infrastructure layer that makes clinical AI actually usable at scale.
