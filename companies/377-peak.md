# Peak

**One-Liner:** AI analytics for B2B product-led growth

**Description:** Peak provides an AI-powered analytics platform designed for B2B companies using product-led growth strategies. Their platform automatically tracks user activation, engagement, and conversion patterns across self-serve product experiences, identifying the specific actions and features that separate users who convert to paid from those who churn. Peak's AI generates user segments based on behavioral patterns rather than demographic data, recommends personalized in-product experiences for each segment, and predicts which free users are most likely to convert. The platform provides real-time dashboards that track PLG metrics — time-to-value, activation rate, product-qualified leads, and expansion revenue. Growth teams use Peak to systematically optimize their self-serve funnel with data-driven experiments.
