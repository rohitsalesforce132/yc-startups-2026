# Advanced Metal Research

**One-Liner:** Machine intelligence for American welding

**Description:** Advanced Metal Research applies machine intelligence to industrial welding processes, helping American manufacturers produce higher quality welds consistently. Their AI system uses real-time sensor data — arc voltage, wire feed speed, weld pool imagery, and acoustic signatures — to monitor and optimize welding parameters on the fly. The platform learns optimal settings for each joint configuration, material type, and position, creating a knowledge base that captures expertise from master welders. Advanced Metal Research's technology reduces weld defects by 60%, increases throughput by optimizing travel speed, and enables less experienced welders to produce expert-level results through AI guidance and real-time quality feedback.
