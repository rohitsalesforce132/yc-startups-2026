# Milestone

**One-Liner:** AI project cost estimation for software

**Description:** Milestone provides an AI-powered project estimation platform that helps software agencies and consulting companies generate accurate project proposals and cost estimates. Their platform analyzes project requirements, compares them against a database of completed projects, and generates detailed estimates broken down by phase, role, and timeline. Milestone's AI accounts for common estimation blind spots — requirements ambiguity, technical complexity, integration challenges, and testing overhead — that cause projects to overrun. The platform generates client-ready proposals with confidence ranges, risk factors, and assumptions documented. Agencies use Milestone to improve estimate accuracy by 40%, winning more deals with competitive pricing while protecting margins from scope creep caused by underestimation.
