# BioStack Platforms

**One-Liner:** Real-world training for healthcare AI

**Description:** BioStack Platforms provides the infrastructure for training and validating healthcare AI models using real-world clinical data at scale. Their platform ingests de-identified patient records, imaging studies, lab results, and treatment outcomes from hospital networks, creating high-fidelity training datasets that reflect actual clinical complexity. BioStack handles the full pipeline from data acquisition and de-identification through annotation, model training, and validation against clinical outcomes. This enables AI companies to build models that generalize beyond curated research datasets to the messy reality of everyday healthcare delivery across diverse patient populations.
