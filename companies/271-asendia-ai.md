# Asendia AI

**One-Liner:** AI recruiters for staffing

**Description:** Asendia AI deploys AI recruiting agents that handle the entire staffing lifecycle for recruitment agencies and enterprise hiring teams. Their autonomous agents source candidates across job boards and professional networks, conduct initial screening through conversational AI interviews, assess technical skills through automated assessments, and present ranked candidate shortlists to hiring managers. Asendia's agents operate 24/7, engaging candidates across time zones and responding to applications within minutes instead of days. The platform learns from hiring outcomes to continuously improve its matching algorithms. Staffing agencies using Asendia double their placement rates while reducing cost-per-hire by 60%, enabling them to serve more clients without adding recruiters.
