# Zealous

**One-Liner:** AI employee engagement platform

**Description:** Zealous provides an AI-powered employee engagement platform that measures, analyzes, and improves workplace satisfaction and team morale in real time. Their platform uses brief, AI-generated pulse surveys, communication pattern analysis, and behavioral signals to create a continuous engagement picture rather than relying on annual surveys. Zealous's AI identifies disengaged employees and teams at risk of turnover weeks before resignation letters appear, recommending specific interventions based on what's driving disengagement. The platform handles feedback collection, recognition programs, and team health dashboards. HR teams use Zealous to proactively address engagement issues rather than discovering them during exit interviews, reducing voluntary turnover by 15-25%.
