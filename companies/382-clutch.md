# Clutch

**One-Liner:** AI customer onboarding automation

**Description:** Clutch provides an AI-powered customer onboarding automation platform that helps B2B companies deliver consistent, personalized onboarding experiences at scale. Their platform creates dynamic onboarding workflows that adapt based on customer segment, product configuration, and user behavior during the first critical weeks. Clutch's AI monitors onboarding progress across all accounts, identifying customers who are falling behind on key activation milestones and automatically triggering personalized interventions — additional training content, proactive support outreach, or executive check-ins. The platform provides onboarding analytics that show which steps correlate with long-term retention and which create unnecessary friction. Customer success teams use Clutch to ensure every customer reaches their first value moment quickly, reducing time-to-value by 50% and improving first-year retention.
