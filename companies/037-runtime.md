## What They Do

Runtime provides sandboxed AI coding agents that any team member can use — not just engineers. Their platform lets product managers, designers, and support staff describe what they want built or fixed, and the AI coding agent implements it safely in a sandbox. Changes are reviewed by engineers before merging. This means non-technical team members can fix typos, update copy, add simple features, and fix bugs without waiting for engineering bandwidth. For engineering teams, it's like having 10 junior developers who never sleep.
