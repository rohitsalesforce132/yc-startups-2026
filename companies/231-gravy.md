# Gravy

**One-Liner:** AI agent for personal finances

**Description:** Gravy is an AI personal finance agent that actively manages users' money through intelligent automation. Unlike passive budgeting apps that simply show where money went, Gravy takes action — automatically moving money between accounts to optimize interest, scheduling bill payments to avoid late fees, negotiating subscription costs, and suggesting spending adjustments based on income patterns. The AI learns each user's financial behavior, goals, and cash flow patterns to provide hyper-personalized recommendations. Gravy connects to all financial accounts via secure APIs and can execute approved actions autonomously, functioning as a 24/7 financial assistant that makes users wealthier through thousands of micro-optimizations.
