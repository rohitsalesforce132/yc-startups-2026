# Spoke AI

**One-Liner:** AI internal communications platform

**Description:** Spoke AI provides an AI-powered internal communications platform that helps companies manage the overwhelming flow of information across Slack, Teams, email, and internal wikis. Their AI automatically summarizes long threads, highlights action items, routes important updates to the right people, and creates searchable knowledge from transient conversations. Spoke's AI assistant answers employee questions about company policies, project status, and tribal knowledge by synthesizing information from across all internal systems. The platform reduces the information overload that plagues modern workplaces, cutting down the 2+ hours employees spend daily searching for information across disconnected tools and channels.
