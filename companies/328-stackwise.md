# Stackwise

**One-Liner:** AI technical due diligence platform

**Description:** Stackwise provides an AI-powered technical due diligence platform that helps investors, acquirers, and boards evaluate the technology assets, architecture, and engineering quality of target companies. Their platform automatically scans codebases, infrastructure configurations, and engineering processes to produce comprehensive technical health reports — code quality scores, security vulnerability assessments, architectural complexity analysis, and technical debt estimates. Stackwise's AI benchmarks engineering teams against industry standards, identifies key-person dependencies, and assesses scalability of the technology stack. Investors use Stackwise to evaluate technical risk in deals in days instead of hiring expensive consulting firms for multi-week technical assessments.
