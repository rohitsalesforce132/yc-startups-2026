# Alloy

**One-Liner:** AI supply chain visibility platform

**Description:** Alloy provides an AI-powered supply chain visibility platform that gives manufacturers and retailers real-time insight into their entire supply chain from raw materials through finished goods delivery. Their platform aggregates data from suppliers, logistics providers, warehouses, and IoT sensors into a unified control tower that tracks every order, shipment, and inventory position globally. Alloy's AI predicts supply chain disruptions before they impact production — port congestion, weather events, supplier financial distress, and geopolitical risks. The platform automatically suggests mitigation actions like alternate sourcing, inventory repositioning, and logistics rerouting. Supply chain teams use Alloy to move from reactive firefighting to proactive risk management across their global operations.
