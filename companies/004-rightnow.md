# RightNow

**Batch:** F26
**Industry:** B2B Infrastructure
**Tags:** hardware, ai, chips
**One-liner:** Enabling Model-Hardware Co-Design at Scale

## What They Do

RightNow builds tools that let AI companies co-design their models and custom chips together instead of sequentially. Currently, AI models are designed first, then hardware engineers try to make chips that run them efficiently. This is suboptimal. RightNow's platform lets model architects and chip designers work simultaneously — adjusting model architecture to fit hardware constraints and vice versa. The result is AI models that run 5-10x faster on custom hardware. For companies building custom AI chips (and there are many now), this is essential infrastructure.
