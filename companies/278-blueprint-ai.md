# Blueprint AI

**One-Liner:** AI product management copilot

**Description:** Blueprint AI provides an AI copilot for product managers that automates the documentation-heavy aspects of product development — writing PRDs, creating user stories, analyzing feedback, and tracking feature requests. Their platform ingests customer support tickets, sales calls, user interviews, and analytics data to surface the most impactful feature opportunities. Blueprint's AI generates structured product requirements documents from rough notes, creates acceptance criteria, and maintains living product roadmaps that update based on changing priorities. Product managers use Blueprint to spend more time on strategy and customer conversations instead of wrestling with Confluence pages and Jira tickets.
