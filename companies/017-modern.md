# Modern

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B Operations
**Tags:** it, operations, ai
**One-liner:** The AI-native OS for IT

## What They Do

Modern builds an AI-powered operating system for IT departments. IT teams spend 70% of their time on repetitive tasks — resetting passwords, provisioning laptops, managing software licenses, handling support tickets. Modern's AI handles all of this automatically. When an employee joins, Modern provisions everything. When someone submits a ticket, the AI resolves it or routes it. It connects to Active Directory, JAMF, ServiceNow, and all other IT tools. For IT teams drowning in tickets, Modern is like adding 5 team members who work 24/7.

## Indian Market Potential

Very high potential. Indian IT departments in 500K+ companies waste enormous time on manual tasks. Build for Indian IT service companies (TCS, Infosys internal IT). Revenue: ₹50-100 per employee/month, 10K employees = ₹5-10L/month.
