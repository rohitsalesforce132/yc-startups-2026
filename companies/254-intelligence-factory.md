# Intelligence Factory

**One-Liner:** Human intelligence for robots

**Description:** Intelligence Factory builds a platform that combines human teleoperation with AI to enable robots to perform complex manipulation tasks they can't yet handle autonomously. Their system uses low-latency video streaming and haptic feedback to let human operators remotely control robots in real time when they encounter situations beyond their AI capabilities. Every human intervention generates training data that improves the robot's autonomy over time — a flywheel of human demonstration leading to AI improvement. The platform addresses the cold start problem in robotics, where robots need thousands of examples to learn new tasks but can't collect those examples without human guidance.
