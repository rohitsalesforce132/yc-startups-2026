# Uno Wallet

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Consumer
**Tags:** fintech, payments, ai
**One-liner:** The AI wallet that picks the best card at checkout

## What They Do

Uno Wallet is an AI-powered wallet that automatically selects the best credit or debit card for each transaction. Most consumers have multiple cards with different rewards, cashback rates, and benefits, but rarely optimize which card to use. Uno Wallet connects to all your cards, analyzes each purchase in real-time, and routes the payment through the card that maximizes rewards. Over a year, this can save users hundreds of dollars in missed cashback and points. It's the set-it-and-forget-it approach to credit card optimization.

## Indian Market Potential

High potential. India has 1B+ debit cards and growing credit card market. Build UPI-first wallet that optimizes which payment method to use. Revenue: Affiliate commissions from card issuers + ₹99/month premium.
