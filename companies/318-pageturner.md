# PageTurner

**One-Liner:** AI product analytics and experimentation

**Description:** PageTurner provides an AI-powered product analytics and experimentation platform that helps product teams understand user behavior and optimize product experiences without requiring data science expertise. Their AI automatically identifies meaningful patterns in user behavior, surfaces drop-off points in conversion funnels, and suggests A/B experiments to improve key metrics. PageTurner's platform handles experiment design, statistical analysis, and results interpretation in plain language that product managers can act on immediately. The AI detects when experiments have reached significance, prevents false positives from multiple testing, and recommends the next experiment based on prior results. Product teams use PageTurner to ship data-informed product changes weekly instead of quarterly.
