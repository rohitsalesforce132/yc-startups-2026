# Playabl.ai

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Consumer Gaming
**Tags:** gaming, ai, consumer
**One-liner:** Prompt-to-play AI game builder for everyone

## What They Do

Playabl.ai lets anyone create and play games using just text prompts. Type 'make a space shooter where I dodge asteroids' and it generates a playable game in seconds. The AI handles game mechanics, graphics, sound effects, and even adjusts difficulty based on how you play. You can iterate by saying 'add a laser gun' or 'make the enemies faster.' For non-gamers, it's entertainment. For aspiring game designers, it's a creative tool. For kids, it's magic — describe your dream game and play it immediately.

## Indian Market Potential

Strong potential. India has 500M+ gamers, mostly mobile. Build a WhatsApp/phone-based game generator for Indian languages. Revenue: Freemium with ₹99/month premium, 50K users = ₹50L/month.
