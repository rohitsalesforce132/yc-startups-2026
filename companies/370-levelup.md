# LevelUp

**One-Liner:** AI employee learning and development

**Description:** LevelUp provides an AI-powered learning and development platform that creates personalized learning experiences for employees based on their role, career goals, skill gaps, and learning preferences. Their AI generates custom learning paths from an organization's content library and external resources, adapting difficulty and format based on each employee's progress and engagement patterns. LevelUp's platform handles skills assessment, training content curation, certification tracking, and ROI measurement of learning programs. The AI identifies organization-wide skill gaps by analyzing project assignments, performance reviews, and industry trend data, recommending training investments that align with business strategy. L&D teams use LevelUp to deliver effective training at scale without the manual curriculum development that keeps programs stale and underutilized.
