# Arctic Health

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Healthcare Services
**Tags:** healthcare, credentialing, compliance
**One-liner:** AI-native credentialing and contracting for healthcare

## What They Do

Arctic Health automates the painful process of healthcare provider credentialing — verifying that doctors have the right qualifications, licenses, and certifications to practice. This process currently takes 60-90 days per provider and involves mountains of paperwork. Arctic Health's AI extracts information from documents, verifies credentials with primary sources, tracks expiration dates, and manages payer contracts. For hospitals and health systems, this means doctors can start seeing patients weeks faster and revenue starts flowing sooner.

## Indian Market Potential

Moderate potential for India. Indian hospital credentialing is handled by NMC (National Medical Commission) and is largely manual. Build an AI tool for Indian hospital HR departments. Revenue: ₹2-5L/month per hospital chain.
