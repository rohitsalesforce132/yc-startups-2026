# Pentagon

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B Productivity
**Tags:** ai-agents, teamwork, productivity
**One-liner:** Your AI agents, working as a team

## What They Do

Pentagon provides a platform where multiple AI agents collaborate as a team to accomplish complex business tasks. Instead of one agent trying to do everything, Pentagon deploys specialized agents — a research agent, a writing agent, a data analysis agent, a review agent — that work together like a human team. The research agent gathers information, passes it to the analyst, who produces insights that the writer turns into a report, which the reviewer checks. For businesses, this means AI that handles multi-step work with the quality of a coordinated team.

Very high potential. Indian companies need multi-agent orchestration for complex workflows. Build agent teams for Indian enterprise processes (loan processing, compliance, reporting). Revenue: ₹5-10K/month per workflow, 200 workflows = ₹10-20L/month.
