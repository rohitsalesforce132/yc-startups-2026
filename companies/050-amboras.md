**Batch:** S26

**Tags:** ecommerce, ai-agents, sales
**One-liner:** Let AI sell your stuff on autopilot

## What They Do

Amboras provides AI sales agents that automatically list, price, market, and sell products across multiple marketplaces. Got inventory to sell? Amboras' AI analyzes market prices, creates optimized listings with photos and descriptions, manages bids on multiple platforms, responds to customer inquiries, and handles fulfillment coordination. For businesses with excess inventory, used equipment, or products to liquidate, Amboras is a hands-off sales channel. It's like hiring a sales team that works 24/7 across every marketplace simultaneously.
