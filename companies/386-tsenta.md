# Tsenta

**One-Liner:** We match you with jobs and apply automatically

**Description:** Tsenta is an AI-powered job matching and application platform that automatically finds relevant jobs for candidates and submits applications on their behalf. The platform uses advanced AI to analyze candidate profiles, skills, experience, and preferences, then matches them against millions of job postings across multiple platforms (LinkedIn, Indeed, company career sites, specialized job boards). Unlike traditional job search that requires manual searching, tailoring resumes, and submitting individual applications, Tsenta handles everything automatically. Users create a comprehensive profile once, and Tsenta's AI agents continuously scan for matching opportunities, customize applications for each role, submit applications at optimal times, track responses, and provide analytics on application performance. The AI learns from feedback (interviews, rejections, offers) to improve matching accuracy over time. Features include automated resume tailoring, cover letter generation, application timing optimization, interview scheduling, and salary negotiation support. Backed by Y Combinator Summer 2026, based in San Francisco.
