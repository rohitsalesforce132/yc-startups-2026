# Pylon

**One-Liner:** AI customer success platform

**Description:** Pylon provides an AI-powered customer success platform that helps B2B companies predict churn, identify expansion opportunities, and automate customer health monitoring. Their AI analyzes product usage patterns, support ticket sentiment, billing data, and communication frequency to generate dynamic health scores for every account. Pylon's agents proactively reach out to at-risk customers with personalized retention messages and alert CSMs when accounts show warning signs. The platform automates QBR preparation, generating account summaries with usage insights and renewal recommendations. Customer success teams use Pylon to manage 3-5x more accounts per CSM while improving net revenue retention through systematic, data-driven engagement rather than reactive firefighting.
