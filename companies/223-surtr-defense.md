# Surtr Defense

**One-Liner:** Open OS for counter-UAS

**Description:** Surtr Defense builds an open operating system for counter-unmanned aerial systems (counter-UAS) that integrates diverse detection sensors, identification algorithms, and neutralization methods into a unified command platform. As drone threats proliferate — from military attacks to smuggling to surveillance — existing counter-drone solutions are fragmented and proprietary. Surtr's open architecture allows defense forces to plug in any sensor (radar, RF detection, optical) and any effector (jammer, kinetic interceptor, laser) through standardized interfaces. Their AI layer provides automated threat classification, trajectory prediction, and engagement recommendations, enabling operators to respond to drone swarms in seconds rather than minutes.
