# Panacea

**One-Liner:** AI-native FDA regulatory services

**Description:** Panacea provides an AI-native platform that streamlines FDA regulatory submissions for medical devices, drugs, and digital health products. Their system automatically generates 510(k), PMA, and IND applications by ingesting product documentation, clinical data, and testing results, then structuring everything into FDA-compliant formats. The platform maintains a living knowledge base of FDA guidance documents, predicate devices, and approval precedents, enabling teams to navigate regulatory strategy with AI assistance. Panacea also tracks submission status, manages FDA correspondence, and flags potential compliance risks before they become costly delays.
