# Arzana

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B Operations
**Tags:** manufacturing, ai, operations
**One-liner:** AI automation for the manufacturing office

## What They Do

Arzana automates the administrative and office side of manufacturing — purchase orders, supplier communications, quality documentation, compliance reporting, and production scheduling. While the factory floor gets automated with robots, the back office still runs on Excel and email. Arzana's AI reads incoming purchase orders, matches them with inventory, generates quotes, tracks shipments, and files compliance paperwork. For manufacturers, this eliminates the office bottleneck that slows down production and causes costly errors.

## Indian Market Potential

Very high potential. India has millions of SME manufacturers drowning in paperwork. Build AI back-office for Indian factories (textile, auto-parts, pharma). Revenue: ₹10-20K/month per factory, 100 factories = ₹10-20L/month.
