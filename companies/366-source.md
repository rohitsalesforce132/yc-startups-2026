# Source

**One-Liner:** AI open-source intelligence platform

**Description:** Source provides an AI-powered open-source intelligence (OSINT) platform that helps security teams, journalists, and investigators discover, analyze, and connect publicly available information to uncover threats, verify claims, and build comprehensive intelligence pictures. Their AI agents continuously monitor social media, public records, news sources, forums, and technical databases to identify relevant information based on investigation topics. Source's platform uses entity resolution and relationship mapping to connect disparate data points that human analysts would take weeks to correlate. The AI generates investigation timelines, identifies key actors and their networks, and highlights information gaps that need further research. Security and intelligence teams use Source to conduct thorough investigations in hours instead of weeks.
