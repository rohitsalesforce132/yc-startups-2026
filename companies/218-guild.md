# GUILD

**One-Liner:** Reshaping defense supply chain

**Description:** GUILD is modernizing the defense supply chain with an AI-powered platform that connects defense contractors, manages procurement workflows, and optimizes logistics for military equipment and supplies. Their system provides real-time visibility into supply chain status, predicts delivery risks, and automates compliance with defense procurement regulations like ITAR and DFARS. GUILD's platform addresses the critical vulnerability in defense logistics — the fragmented, legacy systems that cause delays in getting critical supplies to warfighters. By bringing modern supply chain intelligence to defense procurement, GUILD helps primes and subcontractors deliver faster while maintaining the rigorous documentation and security requirements of military contracting.
