# Clara

**One-Liner:** AI primary care doctor

**Description:** Clara is an AI primary care doctor that provides accessible, high-quality medical consultations through a conversational interface. The system conducts thorough medical interviews, asking targeted follow-up questions based on reported symptoms and patient history, then generates differential diagnoses and treatment recommendations. Clara can prescribe medications, order lab tests, and refer to specialists when needed, all under physician oversight for regulatory compliance. The platform serves patients who face barriers to in-person care — rural communities, uninsured populations, and those with mobility challenges. Clara's clinical reasoning engine is trained on millions of diagnosed cases and continuously updated with latest medical evidence.
