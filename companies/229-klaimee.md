# Klaimee

**One-Liner:** Liability insurance for AI agents

**Description:** Klaimee provides specialized liability insurance products designed specifically for AI agent deployments. As companies deploy autonomous AI agents that make real-world decisions — processing claims, executing trades, answering customer queries, driving vehicles — they face novel liability risks that traditional insurance doesn't cover. Klaimee's policies cover errors, biases, hallucinations, and unintended actions by AI agents, providing financial protection when autonomous systems cause harm. Their underwriting platform uses AI to assess the risk profile of each agent deployment — model type, use case, guardrails, and oversight mechanisms — enabling dynamic pricing that reflects actual operational risk.
