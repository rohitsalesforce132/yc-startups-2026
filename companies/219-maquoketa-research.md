# Maquoketa Research

**One-Liner:** Intelligent one-way attack drones

**Description:** Maquoketa Research develops intelligent one-way attack drones — loitering munitions that combine the affordability of disposable systems with sophisticated AI-driven targeting. Their drones use computer vision and onboard AI to autonomously identify, classify, and engage targets without requiring GPS or external communications, making them resilient to electronic warfare. The systems are designed for mass production at radically low unit costs, enabling swarm tactics where dozens of drones overwhelm enemy defenses. Maquoketa's approach represents a shift from expensive precision-guided munitions to affordable, AI-smart disposable weapons that can be deployed at scale, changing the economics of modern battlefield engagement.
