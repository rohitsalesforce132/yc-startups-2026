# Huscarl

**One-Liner:** Actuarial intelligence for risk managers

**Description:** Huscarl provides an AI-powered actuarial intelligence platform that transforms how insurance companies assess, price, and manage risk. Their system combines traditional actuarial science with machine learning to analyze vast datasets — historical claims, weather patterns, economic indicators, satellite imagery, and demographic shifts — producing more accurate risk models in hours instead of months. Risk managers use Huscarl to run scenario analyses, stress test portfolios, and dynamically adjust pricing as risk factors evolve. The platform addresses the fundamental limitation of traditional actuarial models that rely on historical data assumptions, enabling insurers to respond to emerging risks like climate change and pandemic disruption in real time.
