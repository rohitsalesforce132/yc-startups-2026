**One-liner:** Machine intelligence for American welding

## What They Do

Advanced Metal Research builds machine intelligence for american welding. Their technology brings AI and automation to the construction and manufacturing sectors, which have historically been slow to adopt digital tools. The platform uses computer vision, IoT sensors, and predictive analytics to optimize operations, reduce waste, and improve safety on job sites and factory floors. Advanced Metal Research integrates with existing equipment and workflows, making adoption straightforward for companies without deep technical expertise. With global construction and manufacturing representing multi-trillion dollar markets with single-digit tech adoption rates, the opportunity for digitization is enormous.
