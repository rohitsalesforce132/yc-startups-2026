# Cover AI

**One-Liner:** AI insurance comparison and brokerage

**Description:** Cover AI provides an AI-powered insurance comparison and brokerage platform that helps businesses find the right insurance coverage at the best price. Their AI analyzes a company's risk profile, industry, and operations to recommend appropriate coverage levels across liability, property, cyber, workers comp, and specialty lines. Cover's platform shops quotes across dozens of carriers simultaneously, comparing not just price but coverage quality, exclusions, and claims handling reputation. The AI identifies coverage gaps in existing policies and recommends additional protections based on industry-specific risk patterns. Insurance buyers use Cover to cut broker fees by 50% while getting better coverage recommendations than traditional brokers provide through manual comparison.
