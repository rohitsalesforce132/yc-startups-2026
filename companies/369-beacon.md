# Beacon

**One-Liner:** AI monitoring and alerting platform

**Description:** Beacon provides an AI-powered monitoring and alerting platform that uses machine learning to dramatically reduce alert noise while catching the incidents that actually matter. Their platform ingests metrics, logs, and traces from infrastructure and applications, using AI to correlate related signals and suppress redundant alerts that overwhelm on-call engineers. Beacon's AI learns normal system behavior patterns, detects anomalies that indicate real problems, and generates natural language incident summaries with probable root causes and suggested remediation steps. The platform provides intelligent escalation that routes incidents to the right team based on the affected service and historical resolution patterns. SRE teams use Beacon to reduce alert fatigue by 90% while improving incident detection speed.
