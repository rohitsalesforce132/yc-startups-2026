# FleetMind

**One-Liner:** AI fleet management platform

**Description:** FleetMind provides an AI-powered fleet management platform that helps logistics companies optimize vehicle utilization, reduce fuel costs, and predict maintenance needs across their truck and van fleets. Their AI analyzes GPS data, fuel consumption, driver behavior, and vehicle diagnostics to identify optimization opportunities that reduce operating costs by 15-20%. FleetMind predicts vehicle breakdowns from sensor patterns, schedules preventive maintenance during optimal windows, and routes vehicles to minimize empty miles. The platform provides driver scorecards with coaching recommendations, monitors compliance with hours-of-service regulations, and generates automated safety reports. Fleet managers use FleetMind to manage larger fleets with fewer staff while improving safety and profitability metrics.
