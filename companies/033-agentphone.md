# AgentPhone

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B Infrastructure
**Tags:** telecom, ai-agents, voice
**One-liner:** Phone Numbers for AI Agents

## What They Do

AgentPhone gives AI agents their own phone numbers so they can make and receive calls as autonomous entities. As voice AI agents handle customer service, sales calls, and appointment scheduling, they need dedicated phone infrastructure. AgentPhone provides call routing, transcription, voicemail, and SMS as an API. Your AI agent gets a real phone number, handles calls naturally, and integrates with your existing telephony stack. Twilio built phone infrastructure for humans; AgentPhone does it for AI agents.
