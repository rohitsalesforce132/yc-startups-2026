# Rafter

**One-Liner:** AI procurement for construction materials

**Description:** Rafter provides an AI-powered procurement platform that helps construction companies source, price, and purchase building materials more efficiently. Their platform aggregates pricing from hundreds of suppliers across categories — steel, cement, lumber, electrical, plumbing — providing real-time market pricing that general contractors use to negotiate better deals. Rafter's AI predicts material price trends based on commodity markets, seasonal patterns, and supply chain signals, recommending optimal purchasing timing to lock in favorable prices. The platform handles purchase order creation, delivery scheduling, quantity tracking, and invoice reconciliation across multiple job sites. Construction procurement teams use Rafter to reduce material costs by 8-12% while eliminating the manual supplier management that consumes their days.
