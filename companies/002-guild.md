# GUILD

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Industrials Defense
**Tags:** defense, supply-chain, manufacturing
**One-liner:** Neoprime Reshaping Defense Supply Chain

## What They Do

GUILD modernizes the defense supply chain using AI to predict demand, optimize inventory, and connect manufacturers with military procurement. The US defense supply chain is notoriously slow — parts take months to source, inventory is tracked on spreadsheets, and suppliers are disconnected. GUILD's platform uses AI to forecast what parts will be needed, pre-qualify suppliers, and automate procurement workflows. For defense contractors, this means faster delivery times and fewer stockouts. For the military, it means readiness when it matters.

## Indian Market Potential

Not viable for Indian market directly due to defense procurement regulations. But the supply chain optimization pattern could apply to Indian pharmaceutical or automotive supply chains. Revenue: ₹3-8L/month as a consulting + SaaS hybrid.
