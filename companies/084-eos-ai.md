## What They Do

Eos AI develops autonomous os for healthcare. Their technology combines hardware engineering with sophisticated AI software to create systems that operate autonomously in real-world environments. The platform uses computer vision, sensor fusion, and real-time decision-making to handle tasks that are dangerous, repetitive, or require superhuman precision. Eos AI addresses critical gaps in the market where labor shortages and safety concerns make human-only operations unsustainable. The company's approach prioritizes reliability and cost-effectiveness, making advanced automation accessible to organizations of all sizes.
