# Elyra

**One-Liner:** AI reservation for restaurants

**Description:** Elyra provides an AI-powered reservation and guest management platform for restaurants that optimizes table turnover, waitlists, and revenue per seat. Their system uses machine learning to predict no-shows, optimize table assignments based on party size and dining duration patterns, and dynamically manage waitlists during peak hours. Elyra's AI automatically sends personalized confirmations and reminders through SMS and email, reducing no-show rates by 30-40%. The platform also captures guest preferences, dietary restrictions, and special occasion dates, enabling personalized experiences on return visits. Restaurants use Elyra to seat more guests per night while improving the dining experience through shorter waits and better-informed service.
