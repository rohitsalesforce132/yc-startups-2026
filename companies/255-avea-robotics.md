# Avea Robotics

**One-Liner:** Control robots from anywhere

**Description:** Avea Robotics provides a cloud-based platform that enables remote monitoring, control, and management of industrial robots from anywhere in the world. Their system connects to robots across different manufacturers and models through a universal adapter layer, providing a unified dashboard for fleet management, real-time telemetry, remote diagnostics, and over-the-air updates. Factory managers use Avea to monitor robot performance across multiple facilities, diagnose issues remotely, and deploy new programs without traveling to each site. The platform's low-latency control capabilities even enable remote operation for maintenance and setup tasks, reducing the need for specialized on-site robotics engineers at every facility.
