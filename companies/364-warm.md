# Warm

**One-Liner:** AI outbound sales warm-up

**Description:** Warm provides an AI-powered email warm-up and deliverability platform that ensures sales outreach emails actually reach prospects' inboxes rather than landing in spam. Their platform manages the complex process of building sender reputation across email service providers through AI-controlled warm-up sequences, engagement optimization, and deliverability monitoring. Warm's AI generates natural-looking email patterns, manages reply rates through smart routing, and monitors blacklists and spam trap hits in real time. The platform provides actionable recommendations for improving domain reputation, authentication setup (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), and content optimization to avoid spam filters. Sales teams use Warm to double their email deliverability rates, ensuring their outreach investments actually reach target prospects.
