# AquaShield

**One-Liner:** AI water leak detection

**Description:** AquaShield uses AI-powered sensors and analytics to detect water leaks in commercial and residential buildings before they cause significant damage. Their platform combines IoT moisture sensors, flow meters, and acoustic leak detectors with machine learning models that identify anomalous water usage patterns indicating leaks. The system provides real-time alerts with precise leak location, estimated damage potential, and automated shutoff valve control to prevent catastrophic flooding. AquaShield's predictive analytics also identify aging infrastructure at risk of failure, enabling property managers to schedule proactive maintenance. The platform serves property management companies, hotels, hospitals, and commercial real estate portfolios.
