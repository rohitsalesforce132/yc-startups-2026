# TalentGraph

**One-Liner:** AI skills mapping for workforce planning

**Description:** TalentGraph provides an AI-powered skills mapping and workforce planning platform that helps enterprises understand the skills they have, identify gaps, and plan for future talent needs. Their AI analyzes employee profiles, project histories, training records, and performance data to build a dynamic skills graph of the entire organization. TalentGraph identifies hidden expertise, recommends internal mobility opportunities, and predicts which skills will become critical based on business strategy. HR leaders use the platform for succession planning, learning program design, and strategic workforce planning. The AI continuously updates skills assessments based on new project assignments, certifications, and peer feedback, keeping the skills graph current.
