# Prism

**One-Liner:** AI pricing optimization platform

**Description:** Prism provides an AI-powered pricing optimization platform that helps SaaS and e-commerce companies find the optimal price for every product and customer segment. Their platform uses machine learning to analyze price sensitivity from historical transaction data, competitive pricing intelligence, and customer behavior signals. Prism's AI runs pricing experiments, measures demand elasticity, and recommends price changes that maximize revenue or profit based on business objectives. The platform handles the complexity of multi-product pricing, promotional discounting, and dynamic pricing for different customer segments. Revenue teams use Prism to systematically optimize pricing rather than relying on gut feelings or competitor-following, typically increasing revenue per customer by 10-25% through data-driven pricing strategies.
