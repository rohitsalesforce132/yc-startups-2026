# AgriLens

**One-Liner:** AI crop intelligence from satellite imagery

**Description:** AgriLens provides AI-powered crop intelligence by analyzing satellite imagery, weather data, and soil sensors to give farmers and agribusinesses actionable insights throughout the growing season. Their platform monitors crop health through multispectral satellite analysis, detecting stress, disease, and nutrient deficiencies weeks before they're visible to the human eye. AgriLens generates field-level yield predictions, irrigation recommendations, and harvest timing guidance. Agricultural insurers use AgriLens for objective crop damage assessment, while commodity traders use it for supply forecasting. The platform covers millions of acres across multiple continents, processing new satellite data within hours of acquisition to deliver timely insights.
