# Lattice Health

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B Infrastructure
**Tags:** healthcare, imaging, ai
**One-liner:** The operating system for clinical AI in imaging

## What They Do

Lattice Health provides the infrastructure platform that hospitals use to deploy AI models for medical imaging analysis. Radiology AI models exist for detecting cancer, fractures, and diseases — but deploying them in hospital IT systems is a nightmare of integration, compliance, and data pipeline work. Lattice Health handles all of this: DICOM integration, model serving, GPU management, FDA compliance tracking, and radiologist workflow integration. It's the MLOps platform specifically for medical imaging AI.

## Indian Market Potential

High potential. India has a massive shortage of radiologists (1 per 100,000 people). AI imaging could be transformative. Build deployment infrastructure for Indian hospitals. Revenue: ₹50K-1L/month per hospital, 20 hospitals = ₹10-20L/month.
