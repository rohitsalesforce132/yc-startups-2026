# Momentum

**One-Liner:** AI sales coaching and enablement

**Description:** Momentum provides an AI-powered sales coaching and enablement platform that helps sales leaders systematically improve their team's performance through personalized, data-driven coaching. Their AI analyzes recorded sales calls and emails to identify specific skill gaps for each rep — discovery technique, objection handling, closing ability, or product knowledge. Momentum generates personalized coaching plans with recommended talk tracks, competitive battle cards, and practice exercises tailored to each rep's development needs. The platform tracks coaching impact on actual deal outcomes, showing which interventions improve performance. Sales enablement teams use Momentum to scale coaching across large teams without requiring every manager to be an expert coach.
