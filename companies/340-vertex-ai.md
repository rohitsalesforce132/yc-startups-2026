# Vertex AI

**One-Liner:** AI-powered 3D design platform

**Description:** Vertex AI provides an AI-powered 3D design and visualization platform that enables product designers, architects, and engineers to create, modify, and render 3D models using natural language and AI assistance. Their platform uses generative AI to produce 3D models from text descriptions, sketches, or reference images, dramatically reducing the time from concept to visualization. Vertex's AI handles the technical complexity of 3D modeling — topology optimization, mesh repair, material application, and lighting setup — letting designers focus on creative decisions. The platform supports real-time collaboration, photorealistic rendering, and direct export to manufacturing formats. Design teams use Vertex to iterate through 10x more design options in the same timeframe.
