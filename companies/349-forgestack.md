# ForgeStack

**One-Liner:** AI DevOps automation platform

**Description:** ForgeStack provides an AI-powered DevOps automation platform that manages infrastructure provisioning, CI/CD pipelines, and incident response through natural language commands. Engineers describe what they need in plain English — "set up a staging environment with PostgreSQL and Redis" or "rollback the payment service to last week's version" — and ForgeStack's AI agents execute the infrastructure changes across AWS, Azure, or GCP. The platform learns from an organization's infrastructure patterns and conventions, generating Terraform, Kubernetes manifests, and pipeline configurations that match existing codebase standards. ForgeStack reduces DevOps bottlenecks by letting any developer make infrastructure changes safely through AI-guarded workflows.
