# 9 Mothers

**One-Liner:** AI weapon systems

**Description:** 9 Mothers develops AI-powered weapon systems that combine autonomous targeting with human-in-the-loop decision making for military applications. Their platform uses advanced computer vision and sensor fusion to automatically detect, classify, and track potential threats across multiple domains — ground, air, and maritime. The AI provides firing solutions and engagement recommendations to human operators, dramatically reducing the time from threat detection to neutralization while maintaining human accountability for lethal decisions. 9 Mothers' systems are designed for integration with existing military platforms and weapon mounts, upgrading conventional forces with AI capabilities without requiring entirely new platforms.
