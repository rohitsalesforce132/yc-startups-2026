# Trigger

**One-Liner:** AI event-driven automation platform

**Description:** Trigger provides an AI-powered event-driven automation platform that lets companies create complex automated workflows triggered by real-time business events. Their platform connects to databases, APIs, message queues, and SaaS applications, monitoring for specific conditions and executing multi-step workflows when triggers fire. Trigger's AI handles the complex logic of enterprise automation — conditional branching, error recovery, rate limiting, and data transformation — through a visual builder that business users can configure without writing code. The platform provides full observability into automation execution, with replay debugging and audit trails for every action taken. Operations teams use Trigger to automate the repetitive processes that consume their days, from order processing to employee onboarding.
