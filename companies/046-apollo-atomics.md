## What They Do

Apollo Atomics develops compact nuclear reactors for localized power generation. Their micro-nuclear technology provides clean, reliable power for remote locations, military bases, data centers, and industrial sites where grid connection is expensive or unreliable. Traditional nuclear plants take a decade to build and cost billions. Apollo's compact units can be deployed in months and produce enough power for a small town or large factory. It's the democratization of nuclear energy — clean baseload power wherever you need it.
