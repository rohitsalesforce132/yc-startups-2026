# Aperture

**One-Liner:** AI visual content moderation

**Description:** Aperture provides an AI-powered visual content moderation platform that helps social media platforms, marketplaces, and user-generated content sites automatically detect and remove harmful, illegal, or policy-violating images and videos at scale. Their multimodal AI models identify violence, explicit content, hate symbols, misinformation imagery, and counterfeit products with higher accuracy and lower latency than human moderation teams. Aperture's platform handles the nuances of context-dependent moderation — distinguishing between medical imagery and explicit content, news reporting of violence versus gratuitous content. The AI learns from moderator decisions to continuously improve accuracy and adapt to new content trends. Trust and safety teams use Aperture to protect their platforms while reducing moderation costs by 70%.
