# QuantumOps

**One-Liner:** AI operations for cloud-native companies

**Description:** QuantumOps provides an AI-powered operations platform that autonomously manages cloud infrastructure, application performance, and reliability for cloud-native companies. Their platform uses machine learning to predict infrastructure failures before they happen, automatically scales resources based on demand patterns, and resolves common operational issues without human intervention. QuantumOps's AI agents monitor application health, analyze log patterns, and correlate metrics across services to identify root causes of performance degradation. The platform generates runbooks for known issues and can execute approved remediation steps automatically. Engineering teams use QuantumOps to achieve the operational maturity of big tech companies without building large SRE teams.
