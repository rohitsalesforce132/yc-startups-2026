# Arlo Industries

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Industrials Defense
**Tags:** defense, drones, surveillance
**One-liner:** Passive aerial sensing mesh to track drones and missiles

## What They Do

Arlo Industries builds a network of passive sensors that detect and track airborne threats — drones, missiles, aircraft — without emitting any signals. Active radar broadcasts its position and can be detected by adversaries. Arlo's passive sensors only listen, making them invisible. The sensor mesh uses AI to triangulate positions, classify threats, and provide early warning. For military bases, airports, and critical infrastructure, it's an undetectable air defense system that can see everything without being seen.

## Indian Market Potential

Not viable for solo builder in India due to defense regulations. But the sensor + AI pattern could apply to civilian airspace monitoring at Indian airports. Requires deep hardware + defense expertise.
