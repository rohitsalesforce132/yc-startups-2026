# Zolvo

**One-Liner:** AI servicing for commercial lenders

**Description:** Zolvo provides an AI-powered loan servicing platform for commercial lenders that automates the entire post-origination lifecycle. Their system handles payment processing, escrow management, covenant monitoring, default prediction, and borrower communication using AI agents. Zolvo's models analyze payment patterns, financial statements, and market conditions to predict which borrowers are likely to default months before it happens, enabling proactive restructuring. The platform replaces the legacy loan servicing systems used by banks, credit funds, and BDCs that were built for a pre-AI era, providing real-time portfolio analytics and automated regulatory reporting alongside day-to-day servicing operations.
