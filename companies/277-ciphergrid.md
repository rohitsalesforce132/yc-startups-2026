# CipherGrid

**One-Liner:** AI-powered data catalog for enterprises

**Description:** CipherGrid provides an AI-powered data catalog and governance platform that helps enterprises discover, understand, and govern their data assets across cloud and on-premise systems. Their platform automatically scans databases, data lakes, and SaaS applications to build a comprehensive inventory of data assets with AI-generated documentation, lineage tracking, and quality scoring. Data teams use CipherGrid to find the right datasets for analysis, understand data provenance, and ensure compliance with data privacy regulations. The AI recommends data quality improvements, identifies redundant datasets, and alerts on schema changes that could break downstream pipelines, replacing the manual data cataloging that keeps data teams from higher-value work.
