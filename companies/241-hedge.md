# Hedge

**One-Liner:** AI-native specialty insurance

**Description:** Hedge is an AI-native specialty insurance company that underwrites niche commercial risks that traditional insurers struggle to price accurately. Their platform uses machine learning models trained on alternative data sources — satellite imagery, IoT sensors, social media signals, and industry-specific datasets — to assess risks that lack sufficient actuarial history. Hedge focuses on emerging risk categories like cybersecurity, AI liability, climate-related business interruption, and gig economy worker coverage. The entire insurance lifecycle from application to claims is handled through AI agents, with human oversight only for complex claims, enabling rapid growth without the massive operational overhead of traditional specialty insurers.
