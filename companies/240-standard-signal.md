# Standard Signal

**One-Liner:** Hedge fund where AI researches every trade

**Description:** Standard Signal operates a hedge fund where AI agents conduct all investment research and trade idea generation before human portfolio managers make final decisions. Their AI research pipeline continuously ingests SEC filings, earnings transcripts, alternative data, news, and social media to generate trade hypotheses complete with supporting evidence, risk assessments, and position sizing recommendations. Every trade that reaches a human PM has been thoroughly researched by multiple AI agents playing devil's advocate, stress testing assumptions, and identifying potential pitfalls. Standard Signal's approach eliminates the research bottleneck, enabling a small team to cover the entire market with institutional-quality depth on every idea.
