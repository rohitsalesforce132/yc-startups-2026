# LoopDocs

**One-Liner:** AI document processing for enterprises

**Description:** LoopDocs provides an AI-powered document processing platform that extracts structured data from unstructured documents — invoices, contracts, purchase orders, shipping manifests, and medical records — with near-perfect accuracy. Their platform uses multimodal AI that understands both text and visual layout, handling documents that traditional OCR struggles with: scanned PDFs, handwritten forms, multi-language documents, and complex tables. LoopDocs learns from corrections to continuously improve accuracy for each customer's document types. The platform provides API access for integration into existing workflows and handles document classification, data extraction, validation against business rules, and export to downstream systems. Enterprises use LoopDocs to automate the millions of manual document entries that consume thousands of human hours annually.
