# Voquill

**One-Liner:** AI coworker for pathologists

**Description:** Voquill builds an AI copilot that works alongside pathologists to accelerate tissue analysis and improve diagnostic accuracy. Their system ingests whole-slide pathology images and uses computer vision to pre-screen tissue samples, highlight regions of interest, measure tumor boundaries, and suggest preliminary diagnoses. Pathologists interact with Voquill as a true coworker — asking questions, requesting specific stain analyses, and getting instant second opinions on ambiguous cases. The platform learns from each pathologist's preferences and correction patterns, becoming more personalized over time while maintaining rigorous quality controls and audit trails for clinical use.
