# Mochatrade

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Fintech
**Tags:** trading, fintech, derivatives
**One-liner:** US stock perps for global traders

## What They Do

Mochatrade offers perpetual futures contracts on US stocks for international traders. Many global investors want exposure to US equities but face regulatory, tax, and access barriers. Perpetual futures (perps) let them trade price movements without owning the underlying stock. Mochatrade handles the regulatory complexity, provides 24/7 trading, and offers leverage. For the millions of traders outside the US who want to trade Tesla, Nvidia, or Apple, Mochatrade provides easy access through crypto-style perpetual futures.

## Indian Market Potential

Moderate potential. India has massive retail trading interest (Zerodha, Groww). But SEBI regulations are strict on derivatives. Build compliant Indian equity derivatives platform instead. Revenue: Trading fees, ₹5-10L/month at scale.
