## What They Do

One Robot is building world models for robot evals and training. The platform leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning to automate complex workflows that traditionally required significant human effort. By using advanced AI models, One Robot can process, analyze, and act on data at speeds impossible for manual teams. The system integrates with existing enterprise tools and learns from user feedback to continuously improve accuracy. Early customers report dramatic time savings and cost reductions. The founding team combines deep domain expertise with strong technical backgrounds in AI engineering.

India's manufacturing sector under PLI scheme is growing rapidly. Labor-intensive industries need automation. Government defense procurement creates opportunities for drone/robotics companies.
