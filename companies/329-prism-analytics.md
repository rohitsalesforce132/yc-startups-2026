# Prism Analytics

**One-Liner:** AI marketing attribution platform

**Description:** Prism Analytics provides an AI-powered marketing attribution platform that helps companies understand exactly which marketing activities drive revenue and optimize their marketing spend accordingly. Their platform uses machine learning to analyze the full customer journey across all touchpoints — ads, emails, website visits, sales calls — attributing revenue credit based on actual influence rather than simplistic last-click models. Prism's AI identifies the optimal media mix for each segment, predicts the revenue impact of budget reallocation, and automatically surfaces underperforming channels. Marketing leaders use Prism to justify marketing budgets with data, eliminate wasted spend, and confidently invest in channels that actually drive pipeline and revenue.
