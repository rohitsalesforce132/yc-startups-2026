# Conveyor

**One-Liner:** AI security questionnaire automation

**Description:** Conveyor automates the painful process of completing customer security questionnaires and trust centers that B2B SaaS companies face during enterprise sales cycles. Their AI platform maintains a comprehensive knowledge base of a company's security practices, certifications, policies, and infrastructure details, automatically generating accurate responses to each question in a customer's security assessment. Conveyor's AI understands the intent behind questions phrased differently across frameworks — SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR — and maps them to pre-approved answers. The platform also generates public-facing trust centers where prospects can self-serve security information. Sales teams use Conveyor to reduce security review turnaround from weeks to days, accelerating enterprise deal velocity.
