# Merge AI

**One-Liner:** AI post-merger integration platform

**Description:** Merge AI provides an AI-powered platform that manages the complex process of integrating companies after mergers and acquisitions. Their system handles the massive coordination challenge of combining two organizations — IT system integration, organizational restructuring, cultural alignment, and process harmonization. Merge AI's platform creates detailed integration playbooks, tracks hundreds of parallel workstreams, identifies dependencies between integration tasks, and predicts risks that could derail the merger's value thesis. The AI monitors integration progress against financial synergy targets and alerts leadership when execution is falling behind plan. M&A teams use Merge AI to execute integrations faster and capture more deal value by replacing the spreadsheet-driven chaos that causes 70% of mergers to underperform.
