# DocuRaja

**One-Liner:** Legal docs in 2 min

**Description:** DocuRaja generates accurate legal documents in under two minutes using AI trained on Indian legal templates and case law. Their platform covers the full spectrum of legal documentation — from rental agreements and NDAs to employment contracts, partnership deeds, and company registration forms. Users answer a simple questionnaire, and DocuRaja's AI drafts a lawyer-quality document customized to their specific situation, compliant with the latest Indian regulations. The platform also provides e-signature integration, stamp duty calculation, and notarization coordination. DocuRaja democratizes access to legal documentation for India's 60M+ SMBs that can't afford traditional law firms for routine legal paperwork.
