# Flux Commerce

**One-Liner:** AI-native e-commerce operations

**Description:** Flux Commerce provides an AI-native platform that automates the operational complexity of running an e-commerce business across multiple channels. Their system manages inventory forecasting, dynamic pricing, listing optimization, and order fulfillment across Amazon, Shopify, Walmart, and other marketplaces from a single dashboard. Flux's AI agents monitor competitor pricing, adjust listings for search rankings, predict demand spikes, and automatically reorder inventory before stockouts occur. The platform handles the tedious cross-channel orchestration that consumes operations teams, enabling e-commerce brands to scale revenue without proportionally scaling headcount. Merchants using Flux report 30% higher inventory turns and 20% reduction in advertising waste.
