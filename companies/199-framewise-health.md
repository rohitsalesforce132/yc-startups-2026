# Framewise Health

**One-Liner:** AI-native patient engagement

**Description:** Framewise Health is building an AI-native platform that transforms how healthcare providers engage with patients throughout their care journey. Their system uses large language models and behavioral science to deliver personalized outreach, appointment reminders, care plan adherence nudges, and post-visit follow-ups at scale. Unlike traditional patient engagement tools that rely on generic SMS or email blasts, Framewise dynamically adapts messaging tone, timing, and channel based on individual patient profiles and historical engagement patterns. The platform integrates natively with major EHR systems, enabling real-time clinical context for every interaction.
