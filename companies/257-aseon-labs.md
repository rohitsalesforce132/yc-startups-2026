# Aseon Labs

**One-Liner:** Robotic pitstops for self-driving cars

**Description:** Aseon Labs builds automated robotic pitstop stations for autonomous vehicles that provide maintenance, cleaning, sensor calibration, and charging services without human intervention. As self-driving fleets scale, the bottleneck shifts from driving automation to fleet maintenance — vehicles need regular attention that human mechanics can't provide at 3 AM between rides. Aseon's stations use robotic arms, automated cleaning systems, and diagnostic sensors to service vehicles in minutes. The stations detect approaching vehicles, identify what service is needed through sensor scans, perform maintenance automatically, and log everything to the fleet management system. Aseon enables robotaxi operators to achieve true 24/7 autonomous operation.
