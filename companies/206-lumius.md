# Lumius

**One-Liner:** Fast, smart 3D ultrasound

**Description:** Lumius is revolutionizing medical imaging with AI-powered 3D ultrasound technology that makes diagnostic-quality imaging accessible anywhere. Their portable ultrasound devices combine advanced beamforming algorithms with real-time AI image reconstruction to produce 3D volumetric scans in seconds rather than minutes. The AI automatically optimizes imaging parameters, identifies anatomical structures, and flags potential abnormalities for clinician review. Lumius targets point-of-care settings — emergency rooms, rural clinics, and field hospitals — where traditional imaging is unavailable. Their device connects to a cloud platform for remote specialist consultation and longitudinal tracking.
