# Keeper

**One-Liner:** AI secrets and credential management

**Description:** Keeper provides an AI-powered secrets management platform that helps engineering teams securely manage API keys, database credentials, certificates, and other sensitive configuration data across their infrastructure. Their platform automatically discovers unmanaged secrets in code repositories, configuration files, and CI/CD pipelines, then secures them with encryption and access controls. Keeper's AI detects secret rotation failures, identifies overprivileged service accounts, and predicts which secrets are at risk of exposure. The platform integrates with all major cloud providers, CI/CD tools, and development environments, providing seamless secret injection without developers needing to handle plaintext credentials. Security teams use Keeper to eliminate the secret sprawl that causes devastating data breaches.
