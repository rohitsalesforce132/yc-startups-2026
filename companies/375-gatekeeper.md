# Gatekeeper

**One-Liner:** AI access management for enterprises

**Description:** Gatekeeper provides an AI-powered access management platform that helps enterprises manage and audit who has access to what across their entire technology stack — cloud infrastructure, SaaS applications, databases, and internal tools. Their platform automatically discovers all access grants across connected systems, identifies overprivileged accounts, and enforces least-privilege policies through intelligent access reviews. Gatekeeper's AI detects anomalous access patterns that could indicate compromised accounts or insider threats, triggering automatic access revocation and security alerts. The platform handles access requests, approvals, and certifications through workflows that replace the manual access review processes that most companies struggle to maintain. IT security teams use Gatekeeper to eliminate access sprawl while reducing the operational burden of access management.
