# Nucleus AI

**One-Liner:** AI agent for sales development

**Description:** Nucleus AI builds autonomous sales development agents that handle outbound prospecting, lead qualification, and meeting scheduling without human SDRs. Their AI agents research prospects using company websites, LinkedIn, and news articles to craft hyper-personalized outreach emails and LinkedIn messages. The agents manage multi-touch sequences, automatically adjust messaging based on engagement signals, and handle objection handling through conversational AI. When a prospect shows buying intent, Nucleus's agent books a meeting directly on the AE's calendar with a full briefing document. Sales teams using Nucleus generate 3-5x more qualified meetings per rep while eliminating the tedious manual work of prospect research and cold outreach.
