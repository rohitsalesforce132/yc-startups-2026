# Bonsai

**One-Liner:** AI meeting intelligence and action tracking

**Description:** Bonsai provides an AI-powered meeting intelligence platform that captures, analyzes, and acts on everything discussed in meetings. Their AI joins video calls to generate real-time transcription, identify action items, assign owners, and create linked notes that connect decisions to the underlying discussion context. Bonsai's platform tracks action items across meetings, escalating overdue items and surfacing related decisions from previous discussions. The AI generates executive summaries for each meeting, highlights decisions and trade-offs, and creates searchable knowledge from transient conversations. Teams use Bonsai to eliminate the information loss that occurs when discussions aren't properly documented and to ensure that meeting decisions actually translate into executed actions.
