## What They Do

Klaimee provides liability insurance specifically for companies deploying AI agents. When an AI agent makes a mistake — approves a fraudulent transaction, gives wrong medical advice, sends an offensive email — who pays? Traditional insurance doesn't cover AI decisions. Klaimee fills this gap with policies that cover AI agent errors, hallucinations, and unauthorized actions. As agents handle more critical tasks, the liability risk grows. Klaimee quantifies this risk using AI and offers appropriate coverage. Every company deploying agents in production will need this.
