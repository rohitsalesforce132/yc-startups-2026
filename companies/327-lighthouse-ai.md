# Lighthouse AI

**One-Liner:** AI property management platform

**Description:** Lighthouse AI provides an AI-powered property management platform that automates operations for residential and commercial property portfolios. Their AI handles tenant communication, maintenance request routing, rent collection, lease renewals, and vendor coordination from a single dashboard. Lighthouse's predictive analytics identify at-risk tenants before they default, forecast maintenance needs from equipment age and repair history, and optimize rental pricing based on market conditions and unit characteristics. The platform automates lease abstraction, sends renewal notices, and generates compliance documentation for local housing regulations. Property managers use Lighthouse to manage 3x more units per manager while improving tenant satisfaction through faster response times and proactive communication.
