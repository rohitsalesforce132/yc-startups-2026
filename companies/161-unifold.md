## What They Do

Unifold is building multi-chain deposit/payment infrastructure. The platform leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning to automate complex workflows that traditionally required significant human effort. By using advanced AI models, Unifold can process, analyze, and act on data at speeds impossible for manual teams. The system integrates with existing enterprise tools and learns from user feedback to continuously improve accuracy. Early customers report dramatic time savings and cost reductions. The founding team combines deep domain expertise with strong technical backgrounds in AI engineering.
