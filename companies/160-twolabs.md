# Twolabs

**Batch:** F26
**Industry:** Manufacturing & Robotics
**Tags:** robotics, yc-2026, ai
**One-liner:** Humanoid robots for caregiving

## What They Do

Twolabs develops humanoid robots for caregiving. Their technology combines hardware engineering with sophisticated AI software to create systems that operate autonomously in real-world environments. The platform uses computer vision, sensor fusion, and real-time decision-making to handle tasks that are dangerous, repetitive, or require superhuman precision. Twolabs addresses critical gaps in the market where labor shortages and safety concerns make human-only operations unsustainable. The company's approach prioritizes reliability and cost-effectiveness, making advanced automation accessible to organizations of all sizes.

## Indian Market Potential

Significant opportunity in India where doctor-to-patient ratios are extremely low. AI-powered healthcare tools can serve tier-2/3 cities. Partner with hospital chains and telehealth platforms.
