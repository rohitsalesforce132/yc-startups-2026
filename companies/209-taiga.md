# Taiga

**One-Liner:** Full-stack medical billing

**Description:** Taiga is a full-stack AI-native medical billing platform that handles the entire revenue cycle for healthcare providers — from charge capture and claim generation through submission, denial management, and patient billing. Their AI automatically codes encounters from clinical notes, validates claims against payer rules before submission, and predicts which claims are likely to be denied. When denials occur, Taiga's agents automatically draft appeals with supporting documentation. The platform replaces the patchwork of billing software, clearinghouses, and outsourced billing companies that most practices currently rely on, reducing days in accounts receivable by 40% or more.
