**Batch:** S26

## What They Do

Cohesion provides an AI agent that works alongside public equity analysts and portfolio managers. Instead of manually reading earnings transcripts, building financial models, and tracking industry news, analysts use Cohesion to do the heavy lifting. The agent reads SEC filings, listens to earnings calls, builds DCF models, tracks competitor movements, and surfaces actionable insights. Think of it as a junior analyst who works 24/7, never sleeps, and can process 1000x more information. For hedge funds and asset managers, this means better investment decisions faster.
