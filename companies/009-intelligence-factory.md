# Intelligence Factory

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Industrials Robotics
**Tags:** robotics, ai, manufacturing
**One-liner:** Human Intelligence for Robots

## What They Do

Intelligence Factory builds the AI software layer that makes industrial robots genuinely intelligent. Current factory robots are dumb — they repeat the same motion endlessly and break when anything changes. Intelligence Factory's software gives robots the ability to see (computer vision), understand (AI reasoning), and adapt (reinforcement learning). A robot with their software can handle variable parts, recover from errors, and even learn new tasks by watching humans demonstrate them. For manufacturers, this means flexible automation that doesn't require reprogramming for every product change.
