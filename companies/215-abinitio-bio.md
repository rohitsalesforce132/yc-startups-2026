# AbInitio Bio

**One-Liner:** Intelligence layer for drug manufacturing

**Description:** AbInitio Bio builds an AI intelligence layer that optimizes pharmaceutical manufacturing processes from drug substance production through formulation and fill-finish. Their platform ingests real-time sensor data from manufacturing equipment, batch records, quality control results, and environmental monitoring to create a digital twin of each production line. The AI predicts batch quality outcomes, recommends optimal process parameters, and detects deviations before they result in out-of-specification batches. AbInitio's models are trained on historical manufacturing data across multiple drug modalities, helping pharma companies increase yields, reduce waste, and accelerate the tech transfer from development to commercial manufacturing.
