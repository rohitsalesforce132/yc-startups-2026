# Rudus

**One-Liner:** AI takeoffs for concrete

**Description:** Rudus automates concrete takeoffs and estimation for construction projects using AI-powered blueprint analysis. Their platform ingests architectural drawings, structural plans, and site surveys, then automatically calculates concrete volumes, rebar quantities, formwork requirements, and material costs. What takes estimation teams days of manual measurement and calculation is completed in minutes with higher accuracy. Rudus's AI understands construction drawing conventions, identifies structural elements, and accounts for waste factors and site-specific variables. The platform generates detailed bills of quantities that integrate with major construction management software, helping contractors bid faster and more competitively while reducing costly estimation errors.
