# Mochatrade

**One-Liner:** US stock perps for global traders

**Description:** Mochatrade provides perpetual futures contracts on US stocks specifically designed for international traders who face barriers accessing US equity markets. Their platform offers leveraged trading on popular US stocks and indices through crypto-native infrastructure, bypassing traditional brokerage requirements like KYC complexity, settlement delays, and minimum capital thresholds. Mochatrade uses stablecoin settlement and decentralized custody to provide 24/7 trading with instant settlement, mirroring the always-on nature of crypto markets for traditional equity exposure. The platform particularly serves traders in emerging markets where US market access is limited by capital controls, regulatory barriers, or lack of dollar-denominated banking.
