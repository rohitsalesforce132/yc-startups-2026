# FlowState

**One-Liner:** AI project management for distributed teams

**Description:** FlowState provides an AI-powered project management platform designed specifically for distributed and asynchronous teams spanning multiple time zones. Their AI automatically distributes tasks based on team capacity and expertise, generates standup summaries from activity data instead of requiring meetings, and predicts delivery timelines based on historical velocity patterns. FlowState's intelligent notifications surface only what each team member needs to see, cutting through the notification overload that plagues remote workers. The platform provides async video updates, AI-generated progress reports for stakeholders, and automated risk identification. Distributed teams use FlowState to replace the constant meetings and Slack threads that substitute for in-person coordination.
