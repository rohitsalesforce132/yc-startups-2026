# Patchwork AI

**One-Liner:** AI compliance automation for healthcare

**Description:** Patchwork AI provides an AI-powered compliance automation platform specifically for healthcare organizations navigating HIPAA, OSHA, Joint Commission, and state health department requirements. Their platform continuously monitors compliance status across all locations, automatically generates required documentation, schedules staff training, and tracks policy acknowledgments. Patchwork's AI audits electronic health record access patterns for HIPAA violations, monitors infection control compliance, and ensures required screenings and certifications remain current. The platform generates survey-ready documentation for accreditation visits and provides real-time compliance dashboards. Healthcare compliance officers use Patchwork to manage the overwhelming volume of regulatory requirements that keep them working weekends and still falling behind.
