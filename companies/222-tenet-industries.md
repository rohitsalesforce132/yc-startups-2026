# Tenet Industries

**One-Liner:** Low-cost mass-produced strike drones

**Description:** Tenet Industries engineers low-cost, mass-produced strike drones designed to be deployed in overwhelming numbers on the modern battlefield. Their manufacturing approach borrows from consumer electronics — modular designs, commodity components, and automated assembly lines — to achieve unit costs orders of magnitude below traditional military drones. Each drone carries an explosive payload and uses AI vision for terminal guidance, operating autonomously after launch without vulnerable communication links. Tenet's production methodology allows rapid scaling to tens of thousands of units per month, enabling the swarm saturation tactics that are reshaping military doctrine in conflicts around the world.
