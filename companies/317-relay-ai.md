# Relay AI

**One-Liner:** AI email automation for sales teams

**Description:** Relay AI provides an AI-powered email automation platform that helps sales teams manage high-volume email outreach with personalization at scale. Their AI analyzes prospect data from LinkedIn, company websites, and news articles to generate hyper-personalized email sequences that reference specific events, achievements, or challenges relevant to each recipient. Relay's platform handles follow-up timing optimization, A/B testing of subject lines and messaging, and automatic pause rules when prospects show engagement. The AI learns from open rates, reply rates, and meeting bookings to continuously improve email effectiveness. Sales development teams use Relay to triple their meeting booking rates compared to generic outreach campaigns.
