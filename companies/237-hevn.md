# HEVN

**One-Liner:** Automated USD account for global companies

**Description:** HEVN provides automated USD banking accounts for companies operating globally, solving the pain of receiving, holding, and managing dollar-denominated payments without a US bank account. Their platform gives non-US companies a virtual US banking experience — ACH and wire receiving, USD holding accounts, multi-currency conversion, and outbound payments — all accessible through API and dashboard. HEVN handles the complex compliance requirements of US banking (KYC, AML, OFAC screening) while providing the speed and automation that modern businesses expect. The platform particularly serves startups, freelancers, and e-commerce businesses outside the US that earn in dollars but operate in local currency environments.
