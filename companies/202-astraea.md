# Astraea

**One-Liner:** Agents for clinical trials

**Description:** Astraea deploys autonomous AI agents that manage the operational complexity of running clinical trials. Their agents handle patient recruitment by analyzing electronic health records to identify eligible participants, automate informed consent workflows, manage site monitoring through continuous data review, and flag protocol deviations in real time. The platform acts as an always-on clinical trial coordinator that never sleeps, reducing the typical 30% dropout rate through intelligent patient engagement and proactive site support. Astraea's multi-agent architecture allows different agents to specialize in recruitment, safety monitoring, data management, and regulatory compliance simultaneously.
