# Prototyping.io

**One-Liner:** Autonomous manufacturing for mechanical parts

**Description:** Prototyping.io provides autonomous manufacturing infrastructure that takes CAD designs and produces finished mechanical parts with minimal human intervention. Their platform connects to a network of CNC machines, 3D printers, and fabrication tools, automatically generating toolpaths, selecting materials, optimizing for manufacturability, and routing orders to the best available machine. The AI system handles design for manufacturability analysis, identifies potential issues before production starts, and suggests modifications to improve quality and reduce cost. Engineers upload a design and receive finished parts in days instead of weeks, with the platform managing the entire manufacturing workflow from quote through quality inspection and shipping.
