# Degla Inc

**One-Liner:** The fully autonomous intelligence layer in the sky

**Description:** Degla Inc builds fully autonomous drone intelligence systems that operate as an intelligent layer for aerial operations. Based in Boston, MA, the company develops software and AI systems that enable drones to make real-time decisions without human intervention — covering areas like autonomous navigation, aerial inspection, surveillance, and delivery logistics. Their technology aims to transform drones from remote-controlled devices into self-directed intelligent agents capable of complex mission planning and execution in dynamic environments.
