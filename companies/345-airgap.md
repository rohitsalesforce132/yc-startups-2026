# Airgap

**One-Liner:** AI data privacy and governance platform

**Description:** Airgap provides an AI-powered data privacy and governance platform that helps companies manage sensitive data across their systems in compliance with global privacy regulations. Their platform automatically discovers personal data across databases, file shares, SaaS applications, and backups, classifying it by sensitivity level and regulatory applicability. Airgap's AI generates data subject access request responses, manages consent records, and ensures data retention policies are enforced automatically. The platform monitors for privacy risks like excessive data collection, unauthorized sharing, and retention violations, providing real-time compliance dashboards. Privacy teams use Airgap to manage compliance across GDPR, CCPA, and emerging privacy laws without manually tracking data across dozens of systems.
