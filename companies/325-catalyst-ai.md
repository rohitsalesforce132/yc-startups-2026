# Catalyst AI

**One-Liner:** AI developer productivity platform

**Description:** Catalyst AI provides an AI-powered developer productivity platform that measures, analyzes, and improves engineering team effectiveness. Their platform automatically captures data from code repositories, CI/CD pipelines, project management tools, and code review systems to build a comprehensive picture of engineering velocity and quality. Catalyst's AI identifies bottlenecks in the development process — slow code reviews, flaky tests, excessive context switching — and recommends specific interventions with projected impact. The platform provides DORA metrics, sprint velocity trends, and engineering capacity planning without requiring developers to fill in timesheets or status reports. Engineering leaders use Catalyst to make data-driven decisions about hiring, process changes, and technical debt prioritization.
