# Arlo Industries

**One-Liner:** Passive aerial sensing mesh

**Description:** Arlo Industries builds a passive aerial sensing mesh — a network of silently operating aerial sensors that detect and track ground and air activity without emitting any signals. Unlike radar or active surveillance systems that reveal their position, Arlo's mesh relies on passive detection of electromagnetic emissions, thermal signatures, and acoustic signals from targets. The distributed nature of the mesh means there's no single point of failure, and the collective data from hundreds of inexpensive sensors creates a comprehensive surveillance picture. Arlo's technology provides persistent intelligence gathering that's invisible to enemy detection, ideal for border security and forward operating base protection.
