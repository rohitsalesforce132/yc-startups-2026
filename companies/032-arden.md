## What They Do

Arden automates SOX compliance testing for public companies using AI agents. SOX compliance requires companies to test hundreds of internal controls annually — verifying that access permissions are correct, approvals are documented, segregation of duties is maintained. This is tedious, error-prone work done by armies of auditors. Arden's AI agents connect to company systems, automatically test each control, document results, flag exceptions, and generate audit-ready reports. What takes a team of 10 auditors 3 months, Arden completes in 2 weeks with better coverage.
