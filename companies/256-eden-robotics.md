# Eden Robotics

**One-Liner:** Autonomous services for humanity

**Description:** Eden Robotics develops autonomous robot platforms designed for service applications that directly benefit human daily life — from food delivery and building maintenance to elder care assistance and hospitality. Their robots use a combination of computer vision, LIDAR, and AI navigation to operate safely in human-occupied environments like offices, hospitals, apartment buildings, and hotels. Eden's approach focuses on making robots that are genuinely useful and socially acceptable, with designs that are approachable rather than intimidating. The platform is modular, allowing the same base robot to be configured for different service roles through interchangeable top modules and software packages.
