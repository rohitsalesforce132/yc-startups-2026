# Saffron

**One-Liner:** AI-native recruiting OS

**Description:** Saffron builds an AI-native recruiting operating system that reimagines the entire hiring workflow from requisition through offer acceptance. Unlike traditional ATS systems that simply store resumes and track pipeline status, Saffron's AI actively drives the recruiting process — writing job descriptions optimized for diverse candidate pools, suggesting sourcing channels based on role requirements, automating interview scheduling across time zones, and generating personalized offer letters. The platform provides real-time analytics on hiring velocity, funnel conversion, and candidate experience scores. Saffron's AI copilot sits alongside recruiters, handling administrative work so they can focus on relationship building and closing candidates.
