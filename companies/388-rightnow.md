# RightNow

**One-Liner:** Enabling Model-Hardware Co-Design at Scale

**Description:** RightNow is a research lab building GPU-native AI infrastructure that enables model-hardware co-design at scale. Founded in 2025 by brothers Jaber Jaber and Osama Jaber and based in Delaware, the company builds tools for GPU kernel development and optimization. Their flagship product is the first GPU-native code editor for CUDA, offering smart profiling, hardware-aware agentic AI, benchmarking, GPU virtualization, and a full GPU emulator — all in one integrated development environment. RightNow also maintains a comprehensive GPU specifications database covering 2,824 GPUs across NVIDIA, AMD, and Intel with architecture details, manufacturing specs, API support, and kernel development parameters. Additional products include RunInfra, OpenFang, and Forge. The company addresses the growing bottleneck of GPU kernel optimization as AI models demand increasingly specialized hardware-software co-design for peak performance.
