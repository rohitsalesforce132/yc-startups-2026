# Adialante

**One-Liner:** Cancer screening without barriers

**Description:** Adialante removes the barriers that prevent widespread cancer screening — cost, invasiveness, and accessibility — with a novel AI-powered screening platform. Their approach uses minimally invasive sample collection combined with multi-omics analysis and machine learning to detect early-stage cancers from simple blood or saliva tests. The AI models identify cancer-specific biomarker patterns across proteomic, genomic, and metabolomic data, enabling detection of cancers that traditional screening misses. Adialante's platform is designed for population-level deployment in primary care settings, making screening as routine as a blood pressure check without requiring specialist referrals or expensive imaging.
