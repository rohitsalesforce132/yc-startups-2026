**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B Infrastructure
**Tags:** os, ai-agents, enterprise
**One-liner:** Infinity: the first OS where humans and agents work with no limits

## What They Do

ProjectX builds an operating system where humans and AI agents share the same workspace and collaborate seamlessly. Current OS (Windows, macOS) were designed for humans only. ProjectX reimagines the OS from the ground up: agents have their own desktop, can access the same files, use the same apps, and coordinate with humans in real-time. You can delegate a task to an agent and watch it work in a parallel window. It's the computing environment for the age of human-AI collaboration.
