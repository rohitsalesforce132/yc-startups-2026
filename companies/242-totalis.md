# Totalis

**One-Liner:** Derivative layer for prediction markets

**Description:** Totalis builds a derivative layer on top of prediction markets, enabling traders to create leveraged positions, structured products, and complex bets on event outcomes. While prediction markets offer simple binary or scalar contracts, Totalis adds the financial engineering that professional traders need — spreads, straddles, custom baskets combining multiple events, and leveraged exposure. Their infrastructure handles margin management, automated market making for derivative contracts, and settlement based on underlying prediction market resolution. Totalis transforms prediction markets from retail novelty into a serious asset class by providing the sophisticated trading tools that institutional participants demand.
