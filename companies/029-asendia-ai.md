# Asendia AI

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B
**Tags:** recruiting, ai-agents, staffing
**One-liner:** AI Recruiters for Staffing Agencies and Enterprise

## What They Do

Asendia AI provides AI-powered virtual recruiters for staffing agencies and enterprise HR teams. Their AI agents source candidates from LinkedIn and job boards, screen resumes against job requirements, conduct initial phone screens using voice AI, schedule interviews, and present top candidates to hiring managers. What a human recruiter does in a week, Asendia's AI does in an hour. For staffing agencies, this means placing more candidates with fewer recruiters. For enterprises, it means faster hiring at lower cost.
