# Revnu

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B
**Tags:** billing, monetization, saas
**One-liner:** Everything but the app itself

## What They Do

Revnu provides a complete billing and monetization infrastructure for SaaS companies — pricing pages, subscription management, invoicing, payment processing, dunning (failed payment recovery), revenue analytics, and tax handling. Instead of building billing from scratch or stitching together Stripe + Chargebee + 5 other tools, Revnu gives you everything in one platform. For SaaS founders, this means shipping your product faster because Revnu handles all the money stuff. Think of it as Shopify's billing layer, available for any app.

## Indian Market Potential

Strong potential. Indian SaaS companies struggle with billing, GST compliance, and payment reconciliation. Build a billing platform for Indian SaaS with built-in GST. Revenue: 0.5% of GMV processed, ₹10Cr GMV = ₹5L/month.
