# Skyward

**One-Liner:** AI aviation operations platform

**Description:** Skyward provides an AI-powered operations platform for the aviation industry that optimizes flight planning, crew scheduling, maintenance forecasting, and fuel management. Their platform uses machine learning to predict delays from weather patterns, air traffic congestion, and historical route performance, enabling proactive schedule adjustments. Skyward's AI optimizes fuel loading based on actual route conditions rather than conservative estimates, saving airlines millions in fuel costs annually. The platform also manages crew scheduling across complex regulatory requirements — rest periods, qualification currency, and training obligations. Airlines and charter operators use Skyward to improve operational efficiency, reduce delays, and cut costs across their flight operations.
