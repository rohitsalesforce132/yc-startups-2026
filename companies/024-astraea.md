# Astraea

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B
**Tags:** healthcare, clinical-trials, ai
**One-liner:** Agents that accelerate clinical trials

## What They Do

Astraea deploys AI agents specifically designed to speed up clinical trial operations. Clinical trials involve massive coordination — patient recruitment, site selection, data monitoring, safety reporting, regulatory compliance. Astraea's agents automate the most time-consuming parts: identifying eligible patients from medical records, monitoring trial data for anomalies, generating safety reports, and managing site communications. This can cut trial timelines by 30-50%, getting life-saving drugs to market faster.

## Indian Market Potential

Moderate potential. India is a growing hub for clinical trials (cost advantage). Build AI tools for Indian CROs (clinical research organizations). Revenue: ₹10-20L/year per trial managed, 10 trials = ₹1-2Cr/year.
