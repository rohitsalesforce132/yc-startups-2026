# Waypoint

**One-Liner:** AI financial advisory for startups

**Description:** Waypoint provides an AI-powered financial advisory platform that gives startups the financial intelligence of a full-time CFO at a fraction of the cost. Their platform handles cash flow forecasting, burn rate management, fundraising preparation, and board reporting automatically by connecting to bank accounts, accounting software, and cap tables. Waypoint's AI predicts when companies will run out of money, recommends cost optimization measures, and generates investor-ready financial models with scenario analysis. The platform also tracks key SaaS metrics — MRR, churn, LTV, CAC — providing real-time dashboards that replace the manual spreadsheet work that founders and finance leads spend hours on each week.
