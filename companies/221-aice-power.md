# AICE Power

**One-Liner:** Submarine drones for defense

**Description:** AICE Power develops autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) for defense applications including mine countermeasures, submarine detection, underwater infrastructure inspection, and covert intelligence gathering. Their submarine drones operate independently for extended missions using advanced battery technology and AI-driven navigation that doesn't rely on GPS signals underwater. AICE Power's vehicles use sonar, magnetic anomaly detection, and optical sensors to map the undersea environment and identify threats. The platform supports swarm operations where multiple AUVs collaborate to cover large areas efficiently. Their technology addresses the critical gap in undersea domain awareness that navies worldwide are struggling to fill.
