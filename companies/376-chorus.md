# Chorus

**One-Liner:** AI developer experience platform

**Description:** Chorus provides an AI-powered developer experience platform that measures and improves the daily workflows, tooling, and environments that affect engineering productivity. Their platform collects telemetry from development environments, CI/CD pipelines, and internal tools to identify friction points that slow developers down — slow build times, broken development environments, inadequate documentation, and toolchain complexity. Chorus's AI recommends specific improvements with quantified impact estimates, tracks developer satisfaction through brief automated surveys, and benchmarks engineering experience against industry standards. Platform engineering teams use Chorus to prioritize developer tooling investments based on data rather than the loudest complaints, systematically improving the development experience that directly impacts engineering velocity and retention.
