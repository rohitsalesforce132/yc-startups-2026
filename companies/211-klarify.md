# Klarify

**One-Liner:** AI agent for therapists

**Description:** Klarify builds an AI-powered assistant that helps mental health therapists manage their practice and deliver better care. The platform handles session note generation using AI transcription and clinical language processing, automatically creating progress notes in required formats like SOAP and DAP. Klarify also provides treatment planning assistance, suggesting evidence-based interventions based on patient presentations and tracking outcomes over time with standardized assessment tools. The AI reviews session patterns to flag potential risks like suicidality or deterioration, giving therapists clinical decision support without replacing their judgment. Klarify integrates with major EHR systems and handles insurance documentation requirements.
