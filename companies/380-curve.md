# Curve

**One-Liner:** AI billing and subscription management

**Description:** Curve provides an AI-powered billing and subscription management platform that handles the complexity of usage-based pricing, multi-currency billing, and subscription lifecycle management for modern SaaS companies. Their platform supports any pricing model — flat rate, per-seat, usage-based, hybrid — and automatically generates invoices from usage data, applies prorations, and manages upgrades and downgrades. Curve's AI detects billing anomalies that indicate revenue leakage, recommends pricing plan optimizations based on usage patterns, and automates dunning sequences that recover failed payments. The platform handles tax calculation across jurisdictions, revenue recognition compliance, and integrates with payment processors and accounting systems. Finance teams use Curve to eliminate billing errors and capture 5-10% more revenue currently lost to billing mistakes and failed payment recovery gaps.
