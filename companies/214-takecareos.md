# TakeCareOS

**One-Liner:** Infrastructure for AI-native care providers

**Description:** TakeCareOS provides the foundational infrastructure layer that enables new AI-native healthcare providers to launch and scale. Their platform offers everything a digital health startup needs — EHR integration, clinical workflow automation, HIPAA-compliant data storage, telehealth video, e-prescribing, lab ordering, and insurance eligibility verification — all through APIs. Instead of each new AI health company building these commodity components from scratch, TakeCareOS provides them as managed services. The platform is designed for the emerging wave of AI-first healthcare companies that need clinical infrastructure without the years of integration work that traditionally slows healthcare innovation.
