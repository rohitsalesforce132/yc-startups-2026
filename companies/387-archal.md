# Archal

**One-Liner:** The eval platform for autonomous software

**Description:** Archal provides a comprehensive evaluation and testing platform for autonomous software systems, AI agents, and robotics. As autonomous systems become more complex and deployed in critical applications, traditional testing approaches are insufficient. Archal offers a simulation-first platform that enables developers to test autonomous systems in realistic, controlled environments before deployment. The platform includes physics-based simulators, scenario generators, adversarial testing frameworks, and continuous evaluation pipelines. For AI agents, Archal provides eval suites that test reasoning, planning, tool use, and safety across thousands of scenarios. For robotics, the platform offers high-fidelity simulations of real-world environments with sensor noise, lighting variations, and edge cases. Features include regression testing, CI/CD integration, compliance verification (for regulated industries like automotive and aviation), and performance benchmarking against industry standards. Reduces development time by 30%, catches edge cases that unit tests miss, and provides auditable evidence of system safety and reliability. Backed by Y Combinator Summer 2026, based in San Francisco.
