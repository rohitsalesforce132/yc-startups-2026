# ClearBox

**One-Liner:** AI explainability and model monitoring

**Description:** ClearBox provides an AI explainability and monitoring platform that helps enterprises understand, trust, and audit their AI models in production. Their system generates human-readable explanations for every model prediction, identifies bias in model outputs, and monitors for performance degradation over time. ClearBox tracks data drift, concept drift, and fairness metrics across protected attributes, alerting teams when models start behaving differently than intended. The platform produces documentation required for regulatory compliance in industries like banking, insurance, and healthcare where model decisions affect people's lives. Data science teams use ClearBox to build stakeholder trust in AI systems and catch problems before they become business incidents.
