## What They Do

flowscope uses AI agents to automatically map, analyze, and optimize business processes. Instead of hiring consultants for 6-month process mapping engagements, flowscope's AI watches how employees work (through screen recording, email analysis, and application logs), builds a process map automatically, identifies inefficiencies, and suggests automations. It's McKinsey in a box. For mid-size companies that can't afford big consulting firms, flowscope provides process optimization at 1/10th the cost and 10x the speed.

## Indian Market Potential

Very high potential. Indian companies waste enormous time on manual processes. Build process mapping AI for Indian SMBs, delivered via WhatsApp bot. Revenue: ₹25K per process audit, 40 audits/month = ₹10L/month.
