# ForgePoint

**One-Liner:** AI identity verification and KYC

**Description:** ForgePoint provides an AI-powered identity verification and KYC platform that helps financial institutions, fintechs, and regulated businesses onboard customers quickly while meeting compliance requirements. Their system uses document verification, biometric matching, and database checks to verify identities in under 30 seconds across 195 countries. ForgePoint's AI detects sophisticated fraud attempts — deepfake photos, tampered documents, synthetic identities — that traditional verification systems miss. The platform adapts verification strictness based on risk scoring, applying frictionless checks for low-risk customers and enhanced due diligence for high-risk profiles. Compliance teams use ForgePoint to reduce onboarding abandonment by 40% while maintaining audit-ready KYC documentation for regulatory examinations.
