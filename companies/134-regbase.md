## What They Do

Regbase offers tracking global laws, grants, consultations. Regbase democratizes access to legal services by making expert-level legal work available at a fraction of traditional costs. The system is trained on relevant legal corpora and stays updated with regulatory changes. Legal services represent a $1 trillion global market ripe for disruption, and Regbase is building the infrastructure to transform how legal work gets done across every practice area.
