# General Aviation

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Industrials Aviation
**Tags:** aviation, internet, connectivity
**One-liner:** Fix ATC by connecting aircraft to the internet

## What They Do

General Aviation modernizes air traffic control by connecting aircraft to the internet in real-time. Current ATC systems rely on radar (which has delays), radio communication (which is error-prone), and fragmented national systems. General Aviation equips aircraft with internet-connected devices that broadcast precise position, speed, and intent data continuously. Controllers see real-time 3D positions of all aircraft. Pilots see weather, traffic, and optimal routes. The result is safer, more efficient airspace that can handle 3x more traffic.

## Indian Market Potential

Not viable for solo builder — aviation infrastructure requires government contracts and massive capital. But the IoT + connectivity pattern is interesting for other Indian infrastructure projects.
