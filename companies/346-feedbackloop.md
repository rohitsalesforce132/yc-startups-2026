# FeedbackLoop

**One-Liner:** AI customer feedback analysis platform

**Description:** FeedbackLoop provides an AI-powered customer feedback analysis platform that aggregates and analyzes feedback from every channel — support tickets, app store reviews, social media, NPS surveys, and sales call transcripts — into a unified understanding of customer sentiment. Their NLP models identify emerging themes, track sentiment trends, and quantify the business impact of specific product issues. FeedbackLoop's AI automatically routes feedback to the right product teams, associates it with relevant feature requests, and measures how product changes affect customer satisfaction over time. Product teams use FeedbackLoop to replace the manual feedback tagging and spreadsheet tracking that causes valuable customer insights to get lost in the noise.
