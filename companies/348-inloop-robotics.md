# InLoop Robotics

**One-Liner:** Staff your warehouse with robotic employees

**Description:** InLoop Robotics provides autonomous mobile robots that function as robotic employees in warehouses and distribution centers. Unlike traditional AGVs that follow fixed paths, InLoop's robots use AI navigation to move freely through warehouse aisles, picking items, transporting goods, and collaborating with human workers. The robots can be deployed in hours without infrastructure modifications — no magnets in the floor, no fixed routes, no WiFi upgrades. InLoop's fleet management software coordinates dozens of robots simultaneously, dynamically assigning tasks based on order priority, robot location, and battery levels. Warehouse operators subscribe to robots-as-a-service, paying per robot per month rather than making large capital investments.
