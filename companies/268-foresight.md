# Foresight

**One-Liner:** On-demand consumer insights

**Description:** Foresight provides on-demand consumer research and insights using AI-powered survey design, distribution, and analysis. Their platform lets brands launch targeted consumer research studies in minutes — defining their target audience, crafting AI-optimized survey questions, and receiving analyzed results within hours instead of weeks. Foresight's AI generates statistically significant insights from smaller sample sizes using advanced analysis techniques, identifies hidden patterns in open-ended responses through NLP, and presents findings in interactive dashboards. The platform serves brand managers, product teams, and marketing agencies who need rapid consumer feedback for product launches, ad testing, and market sizing without traditional research timelines and costs.
