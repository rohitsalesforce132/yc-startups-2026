# Huscarl

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Fintech Insurance
**Tags:** insurance, risk, actuarial
**One-liner:** Actuarial intelligence for corporate risk managers

## What They Do

Huscarl provides AI-powered actuarial analysis for corporate risk management teams. Corporate risk managers need to model complex scenarios — what happens to our supply chain if there's a hurricane in Taiwan? What's our cyber risk exposure? Traditional actuarial tools are slow and limited. Huscarl's AI runs thousands of risk scenarios in minutes, incorporating real-time data (weather, geopolitical events, market conditions) to give risk managers actionable insights. For large corporations, this means better risk decisions and potentially lower insurance premiums.

## Indian Market Potential

Moderate potential. Indian insurance companies (ICICI Lombard, HDFC Ergo) and large corporations need this. But requires deep actuarial domain knowledge. Revenue: ₹10-20L/month as enterprise SaaS.
