# Arlo Industries

**One-Liner:** Passive aerial sensing mesh to track drones and missiles

**Description:** Arlo Industries develops a distributed network of passive sensors that create a mesh to detect and track unmanned aerial vehicles and missile threats without emitting signals, providing covert aerial surveillance and early warning capabilities.

**Batch:** S26

**Industry:** Industrials, Defense

**Tags:** defense, sensors, drones, surveillance, military

**Indian Clone Potential:** Critical for India's border security needs, especially along the northern and western borders facing drone and missile threats.
