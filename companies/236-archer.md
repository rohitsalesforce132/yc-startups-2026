# Archer

**One-Liner:** Reward anything

**Description:** Archer is a flexible rewards and incentives platform that lets businesses reward any action, behavior, or outcome through a unified API. Unlike traditional loyalty programs locked to specific platforms or point systems, Archer enables companies to instantly create, distribute, and track rewards for anything from customer referrals and social media posts to employee wellness milestones and B2B partner activities. Their infrastructure handles reward fulfillment across multiple formats — cash, gift cards, crypto, discounts, and custom rewards — while managing tax compliance, fraud prevention, and analytics. Developers integrate Archer's API to add reward mechanics to any product in minutes.
