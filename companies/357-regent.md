# Regent

**One-Liner:** AI contract lifecycle management

**Description:** Regent provides an AI-powered contract lifecycle management platform that handles the full contract journey from request through execution, compliance monitoring, and renewal. Their AI automatically extracts key terms from incoming contracts, identifies risks through comparison with approved playbooks, and generates redline suggestions aligned with company policies. Regent's platform tracks all contractual obligations — payment terms, delivery deadlines, IP rights, and renewal dates — alerting stakeholders before deadlines are missed. The AI monitors contract performance against SLAs and generates renewal recommendations based on vendor performance data. Legal and procurement teams use Regent to manage growing contract volumes without proportionally growing their teams, reducing contract cycle times by 60%.
