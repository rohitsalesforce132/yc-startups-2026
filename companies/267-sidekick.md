# Sidekick

**One-Liner:** AI SMS for deskless workers

**Description:** Sidekick provides an AI-powered SMS communication platform specifically designed for deskless workers who don't have email or company apps. Their system enables companies to send targeted, personalized text messages to field workers, retail staff, warehouse teams, and construction crews for shift scheduling, safety alerts, training updates, and feedback collection. Sidekick's AI handles incoming replies, automatically categorizing responses, answering common questions, and escalating issues that need human attention. The platform supports two-way conversations in multiple languages, making it ideal for diverse workforces. Companies use Sidekick to reach workers where they actually are — on their phones.
