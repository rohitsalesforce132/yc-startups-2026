# Rex

**One-Liner:** AI Operations Partner for Enterprise Order to Cash

**Description:** Rex builds the AI order-to-cash workforce for global enterprises. Specialized agents work continuously across every account, resolving exceptions before cash gets stuck. The problem: order to cash still runs on manual work through inboxes, legacy systems, offshore teams, and Excel sheets. Execution is slow, context is fragmented, and cash gets held up in exceptions. Rex runs the order-to-cash process continuously with agents working every invoice, account, and customer touchpoint so cash keeps moving. Cover every account by reviewing open POs on Ariba, delegating inbox triage, supplier portals, and cash application. Get real-time visibility across the AR book instead of tracking notes in spreadsheets. Get a live view of every customer and receivable. Rex proactively follows up, flags risk, and resolves issues before they escalate. Unlike traditional outsourcing priced by headcount with variable quality and multi-month onboarding, Rex offers outcome and SLA-based pricing with continuous coverage, minute-level triage, live visibility, and consistent auditable quality. Start in days, see value in weeks. SOC 2 Type II pending, GDPR compliant, enterprise SSO with Okta/Azure AD/Auth0. Founded by Lewis Blackwood and Merlin Kafka.
