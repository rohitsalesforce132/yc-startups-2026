# AgentPhone

**One-Liner:** Phone numbers for AI agents

**Description:** AgentPhone provides phone number infrastructure specifically designed for AI voice agents, enabling developers to provision, manage, and monitor phone numbers that AI agents use to make and receive calls. Their API handles the complexities of telephony integration — SIP trunking, call routing, voicemail, SMS, and regulatory compliance across multiple countries. AgentPhone solves problems that traditional VoIP providers weren't designed for: concurrent call handling at AI speed, real-time speech-to-text and text-to-speech integration, and call analytics tuned for AI agent performance monitoring. The platform provides phone numbers in 100+ countries with carrier-grade reliability for mission-critical AI agent deployments.
