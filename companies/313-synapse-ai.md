# Synapse AI

**One-Liner:** AI knowledge management for enterprises

**Description:** Synapse AI builds an enterprise knowledge management platform that uses AI to capture, organize, and deliver institutional knowledge exactly when employees need it. Their system continuously ingests documents, wikis, Slack conversations, meeting recordings, and email threads to build a comprehensive organizational knowledge graph. When employees ask questions, Synapse's AI provides answers synthesized from multiple sources with citations, rather than just returning document links. The platform identifies knowledge gaps where critical information exists only in individual employees' heads and proactively captures that expertise before it walks out the door. Enterprises use Synapse to reduce onboarding time by 60% and eliminate the repeated questions that consume expert employees' time.
