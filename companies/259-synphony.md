# Synphony

**One-Liner:** Robots & software for farms

**Description:** Synphony builds integrated robotic hardware and software systems for agricultural automation. Their platform deploys autonomous robots that handle planting, weeding, crop monitoring, and targeted spraying using computer vision to distinguish crops from weeds. Synphony's farm management software integrates data from robots, soil sensors, weather stations, and satellite imagery to provide farmers with actionable insights on irrigation timing, pest risk, and optimal harvest windows. The system is designed for diverse crop types and scales from small specialty farms to large commercial operations. By combining physical robots with intelligent software, Synphony addresses the critical farm labor shortage while improving yields and reducing chemical usage.
