# YouArt

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Consumer Content
**Tags:** art, ai, creator-economy
**One-liner:** Patreon for AI originals

## What They Do

YouArt is a marketplace where AI-generated art is sold as original, collectible pieces. Unlike stock images or free AI art generators, YouArt treats AI art as legitimate creative work with provenance, scarcity, and value. Each piece is verified as unique, the AI model and prompt used are documented, and ownership is tracked on-chain. Artists (prompt engineers) build followings and sell limited editions. For buyers, it's collecting art from a new medium. For creators, it's monetizing AI art skills in a way that feels like the traditional art market.
