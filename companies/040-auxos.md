# Auxos

**One-Liner:** Digital twins for customer research

**Description:** Auxos creates AI-powered digital twins of customer segments that companies can interact with for research and testing, simulating customer reactions to products, pricing, and marketing without needing to run actual focus groups or surveys.

**Industry:** B2B

**Tags:** digital-twins, customer-research, AI, B2B, market-research
