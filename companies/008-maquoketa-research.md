# Maquoketa Research

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Industrials Defense
**Tags:** defense, drones, military
**One-liner:** Intelligent one-way attack drones

## What They Do

Maquoketa Research develops autonomous one-way attack drones — essentially smart kamikaze drones designed for military use. Unlike reusable drones that return to base, these are designed for single-use missions: they fly to a target, identify it using AI vision, and destroy it. They're cheap enough to be expendable but smart enough to distinguish between military and civilian targets. This represents the future of warfare — autonomous, precise, and cost-effective. The technology draws on advances in AI, computer vision, and small drone manufacturing.

## Indian Market Potential

Not viable for Indian market due to defense regulations. India's drone policy restricts private development of weaponized systems. However, the underlying AI/drone technology has civilian applications in agriculture, survey, and infrastructure inspection.
