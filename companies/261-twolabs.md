# Twolabs

**One-Liner:** Humanoid robots for caregiving

**Description:** Twolabs develops humanoid robots specifically designed for caregiving applications — assisting elderly individuals with daily activities, providing companionship, and supporting healthcare workers in assisted living facilities. Their robots combine dexterous manipulation for tasks like fetching objects, opening doors, and helping with mobility, with conversational AI that provides social interaction and monitors cognitive health through daily conversation patterns. Twolabs addresses the global caregiving crisis where demand for elder care far outstrips the supply of human caregivers. The robots are designed to be warm and approachable rather than clinical, with soft materials and expressive faces that put elderly users at ease during interactions.
