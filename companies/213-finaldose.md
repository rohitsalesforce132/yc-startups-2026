# FinalDose

**One-Liner:** Programmable DNA drug destroying all cancers

**Description:** FinalDose is developing programmable DNA-based therapeutics designed to selectively target and destroy cancer cells across multiple cancer types. Their platform engineers DNA nanostructures that can be programmed to recognize cancer-specific cell surface markers, deliver therapeutic payloads precisely to tumor cells, and activate only in the tumor microenvironment. This approach aims to overcome the fundamental limitations of current oncology treatments — toxicity to healthy cells, drug resistance, and narrow applicability. FinalDose's modular platform allows rapid reprogramming of the DNA constructs for different cancer types, potentially creating a universal cancer therapeutic that adapts to each patient's tumor profile.
