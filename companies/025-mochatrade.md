# Mochatrade

**One-Liner:** US stock perps for global traders

**Description:** Mochatrade offers perpetual futures contracts on US stocks for international traders, allowing global investors to gain leveraged exposure to American equities without needing a US brokerage account or dealing with traditional settlement cycles.
