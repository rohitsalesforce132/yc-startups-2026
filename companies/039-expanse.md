# Expanse

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B Infrastructure
**Tags:** gpu, cloud, ai
**One-liner:** Unlock wasted GPU capacity

## What They Do

Expanse creates a marketplace for unused GPU compute capacity. Organizations buy expensive GPUs but only use them 30-50% of the time — nights, weekends, and between training runs leave massive idle capacity. Expanse aggregates this idle GPU power and makes it available to AI companies at 50-70% below cloud GPU prices. GPU owners earn money on otherwise wasted resources. AI companies get cheaper compute. It's the Airbnb of GPUs — unlocking an estimated $10B+ in wasted GPU capacity globally.

Good potential. Indian companies (Jio, Yotta) are building massive GPU infrastructure. Build a GPU marketplace for Indian AI companies. Revenue: 15% marketplace commission, ₹5Cr GMV = ₹75L/month.
