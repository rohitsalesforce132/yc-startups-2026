# Uno Wallet

**One-Liner:** The AI wallet that picks the best card at checkout

**Description:** Uno Wallet is a smart payment wallet that uses AI to automatically select the optimal credit or debit card at checkout based on rewards, cashback offers, and spending patterns, maximizing savings for consumers without requiring manual comparison.

**Industry:** Consumer
