# TallyStack

**One-Liner:** AI financial close automation

**Description:** TallyStack automates the month-end financial close process for mid-market companies using AI to replace the manual reconciliation, journal entry posting, and variance analysis that keeps accounting teams working late. Their platform connects to ERP systems, bank feeds, and subledgers, automatically matching transactions, identifying discrepancies, and generating proposed journal entries. TallyStack's AI learns each company's chart of accounts and closing procedures, becoming faster and more accurate with each monthly cycle. The platform provides a close checklist that tracks task completion, identifies bottlenecks, and predicts when the close will finish. CFOs use TallyStack to close books in 3 days instead of 10.
