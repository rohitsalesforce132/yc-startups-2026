# Soria

**One-Liner:** AI Bloomberg Terminal for healthcare

**Description:** Soria builds an AI-powered intelligence terminal for healthcare industry professionals — the equivalent of Bloomberg Terminal but for pharma, biotech, and healthcare investing. Their platform aggregates clinical trial data, FDA approvals, patent filings, conference presentations, KOL publications, and drug commercialization metrics into a single real-time feed. Soria's AI analyzes this data to surface investment signals, competitive threats, and market opportunities before they become consensus knowledge. Healthcare investors, pharma strategists, and life science consultants use Soria to make faster, more informed decisions about drug pipelines, therapeutic areas, and healthcare market dynamics.
