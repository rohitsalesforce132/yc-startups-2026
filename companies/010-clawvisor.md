**Batch:** S26

**One-liner:** The Authorization Layer for AI Agents

## What They Do

Clawvisor builds the permission and authorization infrastructure specifically designed for AI agents. As enterprises deploy more autonomous agents that take actions on behalf of humans — reading databases, making payments, sending emails — they need a way to control what agents can and cannot do. Clawvisor provides granular, auditable access controls so companies can safely deploy agents without risking unauthorized actions. Think Okta but for AI agents instead of human employees. Every enterprise deploying agents will need this layer.
