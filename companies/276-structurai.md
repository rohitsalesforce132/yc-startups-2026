# StructurAI

**One-Liner:** AI structural engineering assistant

**Description:** StructurAI builds an AI copilot for structural engineers that automates the repetitive calculations, code compliance checks, and documentation that consume 60% of their workday. Their platform generates structural designs for buildings, bridges, and infrastructure projects by analyzing architectural inputs and automatically applying relevant building codes. StructurAI runs finite element analysis, validates designs against seismic and wind load requirements, and produces detailed calculation reports that satisfy building department reviewers. The AI learns from each engineer's design preferences and local code interpretations, becoming more valuable over time. Structural firms use StructurAI to take on more projects without hiring, while reducing the risk of calculation errors.
