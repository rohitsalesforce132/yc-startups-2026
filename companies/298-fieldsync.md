# FieldSync

**One-Liner:** AI field service management

**Description:** FieldSync provides an AI-powered field service management platform that optimizes scheduling, routing, and resource allocation for companies with mobile workforces. Their AI automatically assigns the right technician to each job based on skills, location, parts availability, and urgency, while generating optimized travel routes that minimize drive time and maximize daily completions. FieldSync's platform handles real-time schedule adjustments when emergencies arise, parts delays occur, or jobs take longer than expected. The system predicts maintenance needs from equipment telemetry data, enabling proactive service calls before breakdowns occur. Field service companies use FieldSync to complete 30% more jobs per day while improving first-time fix rates and customer satisfaction.
