## What They Do

Aster provides an AI-powered research platform that automates the entire research workflow — literature review, hypothesis generation, experiment design, data analysis, and paper writing. Instead of researchers spending 80% of their time on literature search, data cleaning, and formatting, Aster's AI handles these tasks, letting researchers focus on creative thinking and experimental design. For R&D teams in pharma, materials science, and tech, Aster accelerates the pace of scientific discovery by 5-10x.
