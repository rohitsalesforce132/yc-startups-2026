# Avea Robotics

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Industrials Robotics
**Tags:** robotics, remote-control, industrial
**One-liner:** Control your robots from anywhere in the world

## What They Do

Avea Robotics provides remote operation and control infrastructure for industrial robots. Many factories and warehouses now have robots, but they need human operators nearby for complex tasks, troubleshooting, and emergencies. Avea lets expert operators control robots remotely via a web interface — using cameras, haptic feedback, and low-latency connections. This means one expert operator can manage robots across 10 different facilities. For companies with robots in remote or dangerous locations, this is essential.

## Indian Market Potential

Good potential. Indian factories are adopting robotics but lack skilled operators. Build remote operation for Indian factory robots. Revenue: ₹50K/month per facility, 20 facilities = ₹10L/month.
