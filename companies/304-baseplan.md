# Baseplan

**One-Liner:** AI project planning for construction

**Description:** Baseplan provides an AI-powered project planning platform for the construction industry that generates optimized schedules, resource plans, and budget forecasts from project blueprints and specifications. Their AI analyzes building plans to identify task dependencies, estimate durations, and sequence work to minimize project timelines. Baseplan automatically detects scheduling conflicts, resource bottlenecks, and potential delays before they happen on site. The platform integrates with BIM models, pulling quantities and specifications directly from design files. Construction managers use Baseplan to create detailed project plans in hours instead of weeks, with continuous schedule updates as site conditions change and weather impacts are incorporated in real time.
