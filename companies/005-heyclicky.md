# HeyClicky

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Consumer
**Tags:** ai-assistant, mac, desktop
**One-liner:** An AI buddy that lives on your Mac

## What They Do

HeyClicky is a desktop AI assistant that lives as a small floating widget on your Mac screen. Unlike ChatGPT which requires opening a browser and typing prompts, HeyClicky is always there — you can highlight text anywhere and ask it to explain, translate, rewrite, or summarize. It can take screenshots and analyze them, read your clipboard, and interact with whatever app you're using. Think of it as having a smart friend looking over your shoulder all day, ready to help with any task without switching context.
