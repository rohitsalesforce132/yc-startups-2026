# GreenMeter

**One-Liner:** AI sustainability and carbon tracking

**Description:** GreenMeter provides an AI-powered platform that helps companies measure, track, and reduce their carbon emissions and environmental impact across their entire operations and supply chain. Their system automatically ingests data from utility bills, transportation logs, procurement records, and building management systems to calculate Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions using recognized methodologies. GreenMeter's AI identifies the highest-impact reduction opportunities, models the cost and carbon savings of different interventions, and generates compliant ESG reports for investors and regulators. The platform also monitors supply chain partners' sustainability performance, helping companies meet increasingly stringent disclosure requirements and corporate net-zero commitments.
