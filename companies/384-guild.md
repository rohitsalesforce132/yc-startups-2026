# GUILD

**One-Liner:** Neoprime Reshaping Defense Supply Chain

**Description:** GUILD is an AI-native defense prime that builds the operating layer between government demand and manufacturing execution. Founded in 2025 by Kaya Celebi and Erim Gurlemis and based in New York, GUILD automates the end-to-end workflows that move defense production from signal to delivery. As a Neoprime contractor, the company vertically integrates with the defense industrial base to streamline procurement, production tracking, and logistics for military and government contracts. The platform replaces decades-old manual processes with intelligent automation, reducing lead times and increasing transparency across the defense supply chain. GUILD targets the massive gap between antiquated defense procurement systems and modern manufacturing capabilities.

**Batch:** Summer 2026

**Industry:** Government / Defense

**Tags:** defense, supply-chain, AI-automation, procurement, neoprime

**Indian Clone Potential:** India's defense manufacturing push under Make in India creates a massive opportunity. An Indian clone could target the Ministry of Defence procurement pipeline, integrate with Indian OEMs and DRDO labs, and serve the growing private sector defense manufacturing ecosystem (Tata Advanced, L&T, Adani Defence).
