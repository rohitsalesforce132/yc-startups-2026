# Vigilant

**One-Liner:** AI compliance monitoring for fintech

**Description:** Vigilant provides automated compliance monitoring specifically designed for fintech companies navigating complex financial regulations. Their platform continuously monitors transaction patterns, customer behavior, and business operations against regulatory requirements — AML, KYC, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, and state-specific money transmitter rules. Vigilant's AI generates real-time compliance scores, identifies gaps before auditors find them, and automatically produces the documentation needed for regulatory examinations. The platform tracks regulatory changes across all fifty states and federal agencies, alerting fintechs to new requirements that affect their operations. Compliance teams use Vigilant to manage growing regulatory burden without proportionally growing their headcount.
