# Kinect

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B
**Tags:** ecommerce, ai-agents, storefront
**One-liner:** Storefronts that sell to humans and AI agents

## What They Do

Kinect builds e-commerce storefronts designed for a world where both humans and AI agents shop. As AI assistants increasingly make purchasing decisions for humans (reorder groceries, find best deals, compare products), e-commerce stores need to be optimized for machine readability too. Kinect storefronts serve beautiful human interfaces while simultaneously providing structured data feeds, APIs, and agent-friendly endpoints. For merchants, this means their products are discoverable by the next generation of AI shopping assistants.
