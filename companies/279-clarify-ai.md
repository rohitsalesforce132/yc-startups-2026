# Clarify AI

**One-Liner:** AI customer support quality assurance

**Description:** Clarify AI automates quality assurance for customer support teams by using AI to review 100% of customer interactions instead of the traditional 2-3% manual sampling. Their platform analyzes chat transcripts, email threads, and call recordings to score agents on accuracy, empathy, resolution quality, and compliance with company policies. Clarify's AI identifies coaching opportunities, flags compliance violations, and highlights exemplary interactions for training material. The platform generates automated coaching feedback for agents and provides managers with team-level quality trends. Support leaders use Clarify to improve CSAT scores while reducing the enormous manual effort of QA review that most contact centers struggle to maintain.
