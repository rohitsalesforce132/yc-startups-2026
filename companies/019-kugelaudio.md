## What They Do

KugelAudio provides enterprise-grade text-to-speech (TTS) that runs on-premises, meaning data never leaves the company's servers. This is critical for enterprises in regulated industries (banking, healthcare, defense) that can't send customer data to cloud TTS providers like Google or Amazon. KugelAudio supports 25+ languages with natural-sounding voices, runs on standard server hardware, and integrates with existing phone systems and IVR platforms. For global enterprises, it's the privacy-compliant voice AI infrastructure they need.

## Indian Market Potential

High potential. India has 22 official languages and massive call center industry. Build TTS optimized for Indian languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, etc.) for on-prem deployment. Revenue: ₹5-10L/month per enterprise client.
