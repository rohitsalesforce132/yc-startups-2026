# Mount

**One-Liner:** AI-agent insurance carrier

**Description:** Mount is building an AI-native insurance carrier where underwriting, claims processing, and customer service are handled primarily by AI agents rather than human workers. Their platform uses large language models to understand policy applications, assess risk, price coverage, and issue policies in minutes instead of days. Claims are processed through AI-powered adjudication that verifies coverage, assesses damage through photo analysis, and issues payments autonomously for straightforward claims. Mount's approach eliminates the massive operational costs of traditional insurance companies — call centers, claims adjusters, and manual underwriting teams — passing savings to customers through lower premiums while maintaining profitability through superior risk selection.
