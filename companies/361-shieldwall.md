# Shieldwall

**One-Liner:** AI email security platform

**Description:** Shieldwall provides an AI-powered email security platform that protects organizations from phishing, business email compromise, and account takeover attacks that bypass traditional email gateways. Their AI analyzes email content, sender behavior, communication patterns, and attachment characteristics in real time to detect sophisticated social engineering attacks. Shieldwall's models understand the context of business communications — recognizing when an invoice amount is unusual for a specific vendor, when a CEO's communication pattern doesn't match their normal behavior, or when a URL leads to a credential harvesting page. The platform provides one-click reporting for employees, automatic quarantine of threats, and incident response workflows that security teams use to respond to email-based attacks in minutes.
