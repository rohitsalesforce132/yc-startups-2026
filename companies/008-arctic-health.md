# Arctic Health

**One-Liner:** AI-native credentialing and contracting for healthcare

**Description:** Arctic Health automates the complex and time-consuming process of provider credentialing and payer contracting in healthcare using AI, reducing the months-long verification process to days and enabling faster revenue cycle management for hospitals and clinics.
