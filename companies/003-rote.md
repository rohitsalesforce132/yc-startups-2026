# Rote

**Batch:** W27
**Industry:** B2B Operations
**Tags:** insurance, auto, ai-agents
**One-liner:** AI-native insurance department for auto body shops

## What They Do

Rote provides an AI-powered insurance processing system specifically for auto body shops. When a car gets into an accident, the body shop has to deal with insurance companies — submitting estimates, negotiating repair costs, getting approvals, managing paperwork. This takes hours per claim and delays repairs. Rote's AI handles the entire insurance workflow: it analyzes photos of damage, generates repair estimates, submits claims to insurers, negotiates approvals, and tracks payments. Body shops get paid faster and can focus on repairs instead of paperwork.

Strong potential for India. Auto insurance claims processing is a nightmare — India has 500K+ auto repair shops. Build a WhatsApp-based claims assistant. Revenue: ₹999/month per shop, 1000 shops = ₹10L/month.
