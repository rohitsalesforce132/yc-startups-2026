# Ornadyne

**One-Liner:** Robot birds for surveillance

**Description:** Ornadyne develops biomimetic robotic birds — ornithopters that mimic natural bird flight for covert surveillance operations. Their devices are virtually indistinguishable from real birds in flight, using flapping-wing propulsion instead of rotors or propellers. This makes them ideal for intelligence gathering in environments where conventional drones would be immediately spotted and intercepted. Ornadyne's robot birds carry miniaturized cameras, microphones, and signal intelligence payloads, transmitting encrypted data back to operators. The AI-controlled flight system enables naturalistic flight patterns, perching on buildings or trees for extended observation, and autonomous navigation through urban environments without GPS.
