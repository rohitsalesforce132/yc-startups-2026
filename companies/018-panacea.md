# Panacea

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Healthcare Services
**Tags:** healthcare, fda, regulatory
**One-liner:** AI-Native FDA Regulatory Services

## What They Do

Panacea uses AI to handle FDA regulatory submissions for medical devices and drugs. The regulatory process involves thousands of pages of documentation, clinical data analysis, and compliance checks that typically take months and cost millions in consultant fees. Panacea's AI drafts submission documents, identifies compliance gaps, prepares responses to FDA queries, and tracks regulatory timelines. For medical device and pharma companies, this cuts regulatory timelines by 50%+ and dramatically reduces consultant costs.

## Indian Market Potential

Moderate potential. India has its own regulatory body (CDSCO) and growing pharma/medtech industry. Build an AI CDSCO submission tool for Indian pharma companies. Revenue: ₹2-5L per submission, 10 submissions/month = ₹20-50L/month.
