# Commerce AI

**One-Liner:** AI pricing intelligence for e-commerce

**Description:** Commerce AI provides an AI-powered pricing intelligence platform that helps brands and retailers monitor competitor pricing, optimize their own pricing strategies, and maximize margins across all sales channels. Their platform continuously scrapes competitor websites and marketplaces to track price changes, stock availability, and promotional activity. Commerce AI's machine learning models recommend optimal prices based on competitive positioning, demand elasticity, inventory levels, and margin targets. The platform automates repricing across Amazon, Shopify, Walmart, and other channels, executing price changes within minutes of competitor moves. E-commerce teams use Commerce AI to maintain competitive positioning without manual monitoring, typically improving gross margin by 5-15% while increasing sales velocity.
