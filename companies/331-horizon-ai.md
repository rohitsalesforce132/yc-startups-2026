# Horizon AI

**One-Liner:** AI strategic planning for executives

**Description:** Horizon AI provides an AI-powered strategic planning platform that helps executive teams make better decisions by synthesizing competitive intelligence, market data, and internal performance metrics into actionable strategic insights. Their platform continuously monitors competitors, tracks market trends, analyzes customer sentiment, and benchmarks company performance against industry peers. Horizon's AI generates strategic options with probability-weighted outcomes, runs scenario analyses, and identifies strategic risks that leadership teams should prioritize. The platform replaces the months-long strategy consulting engagement with an always-on strategic intelligence layer that keeps leadership teams informed and aligned. CEOs and strategy teams use Horizon to run quarterly planning with the depth of a McKinsey engagement at a fraction of the cost.
