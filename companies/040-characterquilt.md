## What They Do

CharacterQuilt provides AI infrastructure that lets marketing teams generate, test, and deploy personalized marketing content at scale. Instead of creating one campaign and blasting it to everyone, CharacterQuilt's AI generates hundreds of variations tailored to different audience segments, tests them automatically, and deploys the winners. It handles copy, images, email sequences, social posts, and ad creative — all from a brief description of what you want to communicate. For marketing teams, it's like having a creative agency and A/B testing lab in one tool.
