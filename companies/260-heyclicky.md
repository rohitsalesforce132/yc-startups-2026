# HeyClicky

**One-Liner:** An AI buddy that lives on your Mac.

**Description:** HeyClicky is a desktop AI companion designed for macOS that acts as a persistent, always-available assistant living natively on your Mac. Unlike browser-based AI tools, it integrates deeply with the operating system to provide contextual help across applications — helping with writing, coding, research, and everyday tasks. The consumer-focused product aims to make AI interaction feel natural and ambient rather than transactional, positioning itself as the friendly AI sidekick for everyday Mac users.
