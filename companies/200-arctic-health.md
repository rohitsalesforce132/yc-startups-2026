# Arctic Health

**One-Liner:** AI credentialing for healthcare

**Description:** Arctic Health automates the notoriously slow and error-prone process of healthcare provider credentialing. Their AI platform extracts information from medical licenses, certifications, malpractice history, and education records, then verifies each credential against primary sources automatically. What typically takes 60–90 days of manual paperwork is compressed to days. The system continuously monitors for expiring credentials and license status changes, alerting both providers and hospitals before compliance gaps emerge. Arctic Health's models are trained on millions of credentialing documents and handle the complex variability across all fifty US states and international medical graduates.
