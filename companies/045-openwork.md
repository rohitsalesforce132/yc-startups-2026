# OpenWork

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B Productivity
**Tags:** open-source, ai, productivity
**One-liner:** The open source alternative to Claude Cowork

## What They Do

OpenWork is building an open-source AI productivity platform that competes with Anthropic's Claude Cowork and similar proprietary AI assistants. It provides AI-powered coding, writing, analysis, and task automation that companies can self-host. The open-source model means companies keep their data on their own servers, can customize the AI for their workflows, and aren't locked into any vendor. For enterprises that want AI productivity tools but have strict data sovereignty requirements, OpenWork is the answer.

## Indian Market Potential

Good potential. Indian IT companies ( Infosys, TCS) have strict data policies and want self-hosted AI. Build deployment and support services for OpenWork in India. Revenue: ₹5-10L/year per enterprise client.
