# RiskLens

**One-Liner:** AI risk assessment for commercial lending

**Description:** RiskLens provides an AI-powered risk assessment platform for commercial lenders that evaluates borrower creditworthiness using alternative data and advanced analytics. Beyond traditional financial statements, RiskLens analyzes business cash flow patterns, social media sentiment, supply chain relationships, macroeconomic indicators, and industry-specific risk factors to produce holistic credit assessments. The platform generates risk scores with explainable factors, stress tests portfolios against economic scenarios, and monitors existing loans for early warning signals. RiskLens's models are particularly effective for middle-market companies that don't have clean financial data. Banks and credit funds use RiskLens to approve faster, price more accurately, and reduce default rates across their commercial lending portfolios.
