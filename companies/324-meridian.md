# Meridian

**One-Liner:** AI-powered ERP for mid-market

**Description:** Meridian provides an AI-native ERP platform built for mid-market companies that find traditional ERPs too complex and expensive. Their platform handles financial management, inventory, procurement, manufacturing, and HR through an intuitive interface with AI embedded in every workflow. Meridian's AI automates journal entries from invoices, predicts inventory needs from sales patterns, generates purchase orders from stock alerts, and produces management reports from real-time data. Unlike legacy ERPs that require months of implementation and expensive consultants, Meridian deploys in weeks with AI-assisted configuration. Growing companies use Meridian to get enterprise-grade operational control without the enterprise-grade price tag and complexity.
