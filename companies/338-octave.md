# Octave

**One-Liner:** AI translation and localization platform

**Description:** Octave provides an AI-powered translation and localization platform that helps companies expand into global markets with culturally adapted content. Beyond direct translation, Octave's AI understands cultural context, local regulations, and market-specific conventions to produce content that feels native rather than translated. Their platform handles websites, apps, marketing materials, legal documents, and product interfaces with consistent terminology management and brand voice preservation across languages. Octave's AI quality assurance catches mistranslations, cultural faux pas, and layout issues before content goes live. Companies use Octave to launch in new markets 10x faster and cheaper than traditional localization agencies while maintaining higher quality and consistency.
