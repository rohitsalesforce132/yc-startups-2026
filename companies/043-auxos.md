# Auxos

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** B2B
**Tags:** digital-twins, customer-research, ai
**One-liner:** Digital twins for customer research

## What They Do

Auxos creates AI-powered digital twins of customer segments that companies can interact with for research. Instead of running slow, expensive focus groups and surveys, companies talk to Auxos' digital twin of their target customer. The twin is built from real customer data — purchase history, survey responses, social media behavior — and responds to questions realistically. Want to know if your target customer would pay ₹999 for a feature? Ask the digital twin. For product teams, it's instant customer research at zero marginal cost.

## Indian Market Potential

Good potential. Indian consumer companies (FMCG, telecom) spend crores on market research. Build digital twins of Indian consumer segments. Revenue: ₹50K/month per brand, 30 brands = ₹15L/month.
