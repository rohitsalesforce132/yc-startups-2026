# ClaimFlow

**One-Liner:** AI claims processing for insurance

**Description:** ClaimFlow provides an AI-powered claims processing platform that automates the end-to-end insurance claims workflow from first notice of loss through settlement. Their AI agents handle claim intake through natural conversation, automatically extract information from submitted documents and photos, verify policy coverage, assess damages through computer vision, and calculate settlement amounts. The platform routes complex claims to human adjusters while auto-adjudicating straightforward ones, reducing average processing time from weeks to hours. ClaimFlow's fraud detection models flag suspicious claims for investigation while letting legitimate claims flow through uninterrupted. Insurance companies use ClaimFlow to handle claim volume spikes without adding seasonal staff.
