# Kimpton AI

**One-Liner:** AI for portfolio managers

**Description:** Kimpton AI builds a copilot for investment portfolio managers that automates portfolio construction, rebalancing, and performance attribution. Their platform uses AI to continuously monitor portfolio exposures, risk factors, and market conditions, alerting PMs to drift from investment mandates and suggesting optimal rebalancing trades. Kimpton's agents handle the analytical heavy lifting — running factor analyses, stress testing against historical scenarios, and generating client-ready performance reports. The system learns each PM's investment style and preferences over time, surfacing only the insights that matter to their specific strategy. Portfolio managers using Kimpton cover more accounts with deeper analysis, improving both client satisfaction and investment outcomes.
