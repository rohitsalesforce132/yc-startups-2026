# Canopy

**One-Liner:** AI revenue intelligence platform

**Description:** Canopy provides an AI-powered revenue intelligence platform that captures, analyzes, and acts on insights from every customer-facing conversation. Their AI processes sales calls, customer meetings, and support interactions to extract deal intelligence — competitor mentions, pricing objections, decision-maker dynamics, and timeline shifts. Canopy's platform automatically updates CRM records with conversation summaries, generates follow-up action items, and alerts managers to deals that need coaching intervention. The AI identifies winning conversation patterns across top performers and coaches other reps on what works. Revenue leaders use Canopy to get unprecedented visibility into their team's customer conversations and systematically replicate the behaviors that close deals.
