# Gravy

**Batch:** S26
**Industry:** Fintech
**Tags:** personal-finance, ai, consumer
**One-liner:** AI Agent for your personal finances

## What They Do

Gravy is an AI-powered personal finance agent that manages your money automatically. It connects to all your bank accounts, credit cards, and investments, then uses AI to optimize your finances — moving money to high-yield savings, paying off high-interest debt first, automating bill payments, and suggesting budget adjustments. Unlike budgeting apps that just show you charts, Gravy actually takes action on your behalf. Think of it as a personal financial advisor and bookkeeper that works 24/7 to make sure every rupee is optimized.
