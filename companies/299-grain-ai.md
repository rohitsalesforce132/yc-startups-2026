# Grain AI

**One-Liner:** AI agricultural lending platform

**Description:** Grain AI provides an AI-powered lending platform specifically designed for agricultural financing, helping banks and NBFCs underwrite loans to farmers using alternative data sources. Traditional credit scoring doesn't work for India's 150M farmers who lack credit histories, so Grain AI uses satellite imagery to assess crop health, weather data to evaluate risk, market data to forecast revenue, and government land records to verify collateral. Their platform generates credit scores and loan recommendations in minutes, enabling lenders to profitably serve previously unbankable farming communities. Grain AI also monitors loan portfolios in real time, alerting lenders to weather events or pest outbreaks that could affect borrower repayment capacity.
