# RightNow

**One-Liner:** Enabling Model-Hardware Co-Design at Scale

**Description:** RightNow builds infrastructure that enables AI model and hardware co-design at scale. Their platform allows teams to jointly optimize neural network architectures and hardware accelerators, reducing the traditional iterative loop between model development and chip design. By providing tools that simulate hardware constraints during model training and vice versa, RightNow helps companies build more efficient AI systems that are purpose-built for specific hardware targets, lowering compute costs and improving inference performance.

**Batch:** F26

**Industry:** B2B / Infrastructure

**Tags:** AI infrastructure, hardware-software co-design, chip design, model optimization, accelerators

**Indian Clone Potential:** India's growing semiconductor design ecosystem (with companies like Tata Electronics and government chip fab push) makes this highly relevant. An Indian version targeting the domestic VLSI and edge AI market could serve the booming embedded systems and automotive sectors.
