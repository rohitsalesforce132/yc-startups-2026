# Gridlock

**One-Liner:** AI infrastructure cost optimization

**Description:** Gridlock provides an AI-powered cloud cost optimization platform that automatically identifies and eliminates waste in cloud infrastructure spending. Their AI agents continuously analyze resource utilization, pricing patterns, and workload characteristics to find savings opportunities — idle resources, oversized instances, inefficient storage tiers, and missed reserved instance coverage. Gridlock's platform goes beyond recommendations to automatically execute approved optimizations: right-sizing instances, purchasing committed use discounts, cleaning up unused resources, and scheduling non-production workloads to turn off during off-hours. Engineering and finance teams use Gridlock to cut cloud bills by 25-40% without any application performance impact, with the savings appearing directly in their monthly invoice.
