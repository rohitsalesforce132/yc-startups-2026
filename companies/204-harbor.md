# Harbor

**One-Liner:** AI system-of-record for clinical trial data

**Description:** Harbor creates a centralized AI-powered system-of-record for all clinical trial data, replacing the fragmented landscape of EDC systems, CTMS platforms, safety databases, and lab systems that trial teams currently juggle. Their platform unifies trial data into a single source of truth with intelligent data reconciliation, automated query resolution, and real-time trial health dashboards. Harbor's AI continuously monitors for data quality issues, enrollment risks, and safety signals that would take human monitors weeks to detect. The system generates regulatory-ready exports and maintains complete audit trails for FDA and EMA submissions.
