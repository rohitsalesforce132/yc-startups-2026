## What They Do

Hyper builds an AI system that acts as the autonomous brain of a company — coordinating work across departments, making decisions, and executing tasks without human intervention. It connects to all your company tools (Slack, email, CRM, project management) and uses AI agents to route work, follow up on deadlines, escalate issues, and even draft responses. Think of it as a Chief of Staff that never sleeps. For growing companies drowning in coordination overhead, Hyper automates the operational layer so humans can focus on strategy.
