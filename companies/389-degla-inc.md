# Degla Inc

**One-Liner:** The fully autonomous intelligence layer in the sky

**Description:** Degla builds fully autonomous aerial intelligence systems that turn natural language commands into multi-drone mission execution. Founded in 2026 by Aziz Hanafi and Ahmed Zain and based in Boston, Degla serves search and rescue teams, emergency services, and public safety agencies with intelligent drone fleets that operate autonomously. Users can deploy complex multi-drone missions from a single natural language command, enabling rapid response in critical situations where every second counts. The platform handles mission planning, fleet coordination, real-time aerial intelligence gathering, and autonomous navigation without requiring manual piloting expertise. Degla positions itself as the intelligence layer that bridges the gap between raw drone hardware and actionable aerial operations, targeting the public safety and emergency response market where autonomous drone coordination can be life-saving.
