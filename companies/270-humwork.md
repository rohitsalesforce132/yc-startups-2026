# Humwork

**One-Liner:** Connect agents to human experts

**Description:** Humwork builds a platform that seamlessly connects AI agents to human experts when the agents encounter tasks beyond their capabilities. Their system provides a real-time handoff mechanism where AI agents can request human assistance, provide context about what they've tried, and receive guidance that they can immediately apply. The platform maintains a network of on-demand experts across different domains — legal, medical, technical, creative — available to support AI agents within minutes. Every human intervention generates training data that improves agent autonomy over time. Humwork addresses the critical gap in agent deployments where 100% automation isn't yet possible but current systems fail silently instead of asking for help.
