# Shift AI

**One-Liner:** AI workforce scheduling and optimization

**Description:** Shift AI provides an AI-powered workforce scheduling platform that creates optimal shift rosters for businesses with hourly workers — retail stores, restaurants, warehouses, and healthcare facilities. Their AI considers employee availability, skill certifications, labor regulations, predicted demand, and historical patterns to generate schedules that minimize overtime while ensuring adequate coverage. Shift AI handles last-minute changes gracefully, automatically finding qualified replacements when employees call out. The platform provides employees with a mobile app for shift swaps, time-off requests, and availability updates. Managers use Shift AI to reduce scheduling time from hours to minutes while cutting labor costs by 8-12% through better demand-coverage matching.
