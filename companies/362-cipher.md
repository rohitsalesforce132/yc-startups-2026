# Cipher

**One-Liner:** AI data encryption platform

**Description:** Cipher provides an AI-powered data encryption and protection platform that automatically classifies sensitive data and applies appropriate encryption and access controls without requiring developers to implement cryptography manually. Their platform integrates with databases, file systems, and cloud storage to discover sensitive data fields, recommend encryption strategies, and manage encryption keys through a centralized policy engine. Cipher's AI monitors data access patterns for anomalous behavior that could indicate unauthorized access, automatically rotating keys and revoking access when threats are detected. The platform handles the full lifecycle of encryption management — key generation, rotation, distribution, and retirement — maintaining compliance with PCI-DSS, HIPAA, and SOC 2 encryption requirements.
