# Regbase

**One-Liner:** Tracking global laws, grants, consultations

**Description:** Regbase provides an AI-powered regulatory intelligence platform that continuously monitors, tracks, and analyzes legislative changes, grant opportunities, and public consultations across jurisdictions worldwide. Their system aggregates data from government gazettes, legislative bodies, regulatory agencies, and public consultation portals, using NLP to extract relevant changes and assess their impact on specific industries. Companies subscribe to Regbase to stay ahead of regulatory shifts without maintaining teams of compliance analysts reading hundreds of government sources daily. The platform provides actionable alerts, regulatory impact assessments, and automated compliance gap analyses, turning regulatory monitoring from a reactive burden into a strategic advantage.
