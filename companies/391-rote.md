# Rote

**One-Liner:** AI-native insurance department for auto body shops

**Description:** Rote provides an AI-native insurance department that handles the entire negotiation lifecycle between auto body repair shops and insurance companies. Founded in 2026 by Gabriel Castillo and Aditya Mittal and based in San Francisco, Rote helps body shops recover thousands of dollars in denied or underpaid claims. The core problem: auto body shops lose millions of dollars annually because they lack the specialized resources and time to negotiate with insurance companies that systematically undervalue repair costs. Rote's AI system manages claim submissions, supplemental requests, documentation, and back-and-forth negotiations with insurance adjusters — tasks that previously required dedicated staff or went entirely unhandled. By automating the insurance claims workflow, Rote functions as a fully operational insurance department for shops that cannot afford to hire one, directly impacting their bottom line by recovering revenue that would otherwise be lost.

**Indian Clone Potential:** Strong potential in India's growing motor insurance market (largest general insurance segment). Indian auto body shops face similar issues with insurance company underpayment. Clone would need to handle Indian insurance workflows (cashless vs reimbursement claims), integrate with IRDAI systems and Indian insurers (ICICI Lombard, HDFC Ergo), and handle India-specific repair estimation standards.
