# Meterlogic

**One-Liner:** AI utility bill management for enterprises

**Description:** Meterlogic provides an AI-powered utility expense management platform that helps enterprises with multiple locations track, optimize, and reduce their electricity, gas, water, and telecom costs. Their AI automatically ingests utility bills across all locations, identifies billing errors, detects rate plan inefficiencies, and benchmarks consumption against similar facilities. Meterlogic's platform flags anomalies that indicate equipment problems wasting energy, recommends optimal rate plans based on usage patterns, and automates the process of switching providers or renegotiating contracts. Companies with distributed portfolios — retail chains, restaurant groups, property managers — use Meterlogic to reduce utility spend by 10-20% without any capital investment.
