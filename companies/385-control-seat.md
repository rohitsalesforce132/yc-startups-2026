# Control Seat

**One-Liner:** Control and Monitoring of Industrial Operations

**Description:** Control Seat is an AI-native intelligence layer that sits on top of existing SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems to modernize industrial operations. Founded in 2025 by Jack Grodnick and Warren Shepard, the platform combines real-time data, modern software, and AI to turn complex industrial operations into systems people can actually understand — and eventually, systems that AI can manage autonomously. Key capabilities include faster root cause analysis, predictive maintenance, hardware misconfiguration detection, and AI-built visualization views. By layering intelligence over legacy industrial control systems, Control Seat bridges the gap between decades-old OT infrastructure and modern AI-driven operations, targeting sectors like manufacturing, energy, and utilities where SCADA is ubiquitous but deeply under-optimized.
