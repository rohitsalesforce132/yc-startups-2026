# Roster

**One-Liner:** AI compliance for HR teams

**Description:** Roster provides an AI-powered HR compliance platform that helps companies stay compliant with employment laws, labor regulations, and workplace requirements across all jurisdictions where they operate. Their platform maintains a continuously updated database of federal, state, and local employment regulations, automatically assessing each company's compliance posture. Roster's AI flags potential violations before they result in lawsuits or penalties, generates required postings and notices, and manages documentation for audits. The platform handles multi-state and international compliance complexity, providing clear action items when new regulations take effect. HR teams use Roster to replace expensive external compliance consultants with an always-on, always-current compliance partner.
