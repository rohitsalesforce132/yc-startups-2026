# VendorAI

**One-Liner:** AI vendor management and procurement

**Description:** VendorAI provides an AI-powered vendor management platform that automates the entire procurement lifecycle from vendor discovery through contract management and performance monitoring. Their AI agents continuously scan the market for alternative suppliers, benchmark pricing against industry data, and negotiate better terms by analyzing contract patterns across similar deals. VendorAI automates RFP creation, vendor scoring, due diligence checks, and contract generation with AI-suggested terms based on industry best practices. The platform monitors vendor performance against SLAs, flags renewal dates, and identifies consolidation opportunities where using fewer vendors could unlock volume discounts.
