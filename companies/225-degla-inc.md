# Degla Inc

**One-Liner:** Fully autonomous intelligence in the sky

**Description:** Degla Inc builds fully autonomous aerial intelligence platforms that conduct persistent surveillance, reconnaissance, and intelligence gathering without human operators. Their drones use onboard AI to plan flight paths, adapt to weather conditions, identify points of interest, and prioritize data collection in real time. Unlike traditional ISR platforms that require satellite links and remote pilots, Degla's systems operate independently, processing imagery and signals intelligence onboard and returning only actionable insights. The platform supports both fixed-wing long-endurance missions and rotary-wing close-range operations, providing military and intelligence agencies with a scalable, always-on aerial intelligence capability.
