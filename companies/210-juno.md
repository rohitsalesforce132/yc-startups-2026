# Juno

**One-Liner:** AI health assistant for chronic illness

**Description:** Juno provides an AI-powered health assistant specifically designed for people managing chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, heart failure, and COPD. The assistant lives on the patient's phone and provides daily personalized guidance on medication adherence, diet, exercise, symptom tracking, and care coordination. Unlike generic health apps, Juno integrates with continuous glucose monitors, blood pressure cuffs, and other IoT devices to provide real-time coaching based on actual physiological data. The AI learns each patient's patterns, triggers, and preferences over time, escalating to human care teams when it detects concerning trends that require clinical intervention.
