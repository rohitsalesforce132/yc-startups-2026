# Nexus CRM

**One-Liner:** AI-native CRM for SMBs

**Description:** Nexus CRM provides an AI-native customer relationship management platform built from the ground up for small and medium businesses that find traditional CRMs too complex. Their AI automatically captures customer interactions from email, phone, WhatsApp, and social media, building relationship timelines without manual data entry. Nexus suggests follow-up actions, drafts personalized outreach, and alerts sales teams to deals at risk of stalling. The platform generates pipeline forecasts, identifies upsell opportunities, and provides conversation intelligence from sales calls. SMBs use Nexus to get enterprise-grade CRM capabilities without the implementation cost and training overhead that makes Salesforce impractical for smaller teams.
