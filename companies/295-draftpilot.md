# DraftPilot

**One-Liner:** AI contract drafting and review

**Description:** DraftPilot provides an AI-powered contract drafting and review platform that helps legal teams produce better contracts faster. Their AI generates first drafts from deal terms, automatically incorporating appropriate clauses based on deal type, jurisdiction, and risk profile. When reviewing incoming contracts, DraftPilot identifies non-standard terms, flags missing protections, and suggests redlines aligned with the company's playbook. The platform maintains a clause library that learns from approved edits, building institutional knowledge of preferred positions. Legal teams use DraftPilot to reduce contract turnaround time from days to hours while maintaining consistency and reducing the risk of unfavorable terms slipping through during high-volume deal processing.
