# SpendLens

**One-Liner:** AI procurement spend analysis

**Description:** SpendLens provides AI-powered spend analysis that gives procurement teams complete visibility into company spending patterns, supplier consolidation opportunities, and savings potential. Their platform automatically categorizes millions of transactions from ERP and purchasing systems using machine learning, creating a clean spend taxonomy that most companies struggle to maintain manually. SpendLens identifies maverick spending outside contracted suppliers, flags duplicate vendors, and benchmarks pricing against industry averages. The AI generates actionable savings recommendations — renegotiate this contract, consolidate these suppliers, switch to this alternative — with projected dollar impact. CFOs and CPOs use SpendLens to find 5-15% savings hidden in their procurement data.
