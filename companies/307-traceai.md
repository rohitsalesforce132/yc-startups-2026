# TraceAI

**One-Liner:** AI observability for LLM applications

**Description:** TraceAI provides purpose-built observability for LLM-powered applications, helping engineering teams monitor, debug, and optimize production AI systems. Their platform traces every LLM call through complex agentic workflows, capturing prompts, completions, latencies, token usage, and tool invocations. TraceAI's analytics surface patterns in model behavior — hallucination rates, error clusters, cost spikes, and quality degradation — that are invisible to traditional APM tools. The platform supports semantic monitoring where alerts trigger based on output quality rather than just system metrics. Engineering teams use TraceAI to debug agent failures, optimize prompt performance, and maintain reliable AI applications at scale.
