# Beacon AI

**One-Liner:** AI logistics optimization

**Description:** Beacon AI provides an AI-powered logistics optimization platform that helps shipping companies, freight forwarders, and 3PLs reduce transportation costs while improving delivery reliability. Their platform uses machine learning to optimize route planning across trucking, ocean, and air freight, considering real-time factors like traffic, weather, port congestion, and carrier availability. Beacon's AI predicts transit times more accurately than carrier estimates, identifies opportunities for consolidation, and automatically selects the optimal mode and carrier for each shipment. The platform provides a unified control tower for multi-modal logistics operations with predictive ETAs, exception management, and automated carrier communication. Logistics teams use Beacon to cut shipping costs by 15% while improving on-time delivery rates.
