# River Markets

**One-Liner:** Prime brokerage for prediction markets

**Description:** River Markets provides prime brokerage services specifically designed for the rapidly growing prediction markets ecosystem. As prediction markets like Polymarket, Kalshi, and others gain mainstream adoption, professional traders and funds need institutional-grade infrastructure to trade at scale. River Markets offers margin lending, cross-margining across multiple prediction market platforms, real-time risk analytics, automated market making, and execution algorithms optimized for binary options. Their infrastructure bridges the gap between traditional finance prime brokerage and the unique mechanics of prediction markets — including event risk, expiry timing, and multi-platform liquidity fragmentation.
