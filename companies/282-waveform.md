# Waveform

**One-Liner:** AI audio intelligence platform

**Description:** Waveform builds an AI platform that transforms how businesses extract insights from audio and voice data. Their technology processes customer calls, meetings, podcasts, and voice messages to generate transcripts, summaries, sentiment analysis, and action items with higher accuracy than general-purpose speech-to-text tools. Waveform's models are fine-tuned for business contexts — understanding financial terminology, medical jargon, and technical discussions that confuse generic models. The platform provides real-time analytics on voice interactions, identifying customer churn signals, compliance violations, and coaching opportunities. Sales teams use Waveform to analyze call recordings, while contact centers use it for automated quality monitoring across millions of conversations.
