# Clawvisor

**One-Liner:** Authorization layer for AI agents

**Description:** Clawvisor provides the authorization and access control layer specifically designed for AI agents operating in enterprise environments. Their platform lets security teams define granular policies for what AI agents can access, modify, and execute — similar to IAM for human employees but adapted for the unique behaviors of autonomous agents. Clawvisor monitors agent actions in real time, enforcing least-privilege principles and providing detailed audit logs of every action taken. When agents attempt to access new resources or take unexpected actions, Clawvisor can require human approval, automatically revoke access, or quarantine the agent. The platform integrates with existing identity providers and policy engines.
